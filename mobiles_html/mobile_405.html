    Product Description
    Ultra-slim 3D Curved Display

    V40e comes with a 3D Curved Display ensuring a comfortable grip and an immersive viewing experience. A luxury design that is crafted to mesmerize from every angle, V40e is an exquisite piece of technology that marries style with effortless comfort.

    5500 mAh Battery

    Slimness is an integral part of the V-series DNA. With a slimness of just 0.749cm, the V40e is India’s slimmest smartphone in the 5500 mAh battery category. That’s what we call So Slim yet So Large.

    Sony Professional Night Portrait Camera

    With Optical Image Stabilization similar to flagship models, combined with Vivo's newly upgraded ultra HD imaging algorithm, image quality in low-light and backlit conditions has been improved significantly enabling you to effortlessly capture stunning HD night photos. The Sony IMX882 sensor has a super photo sensitivity allowing you to capture more details in low light conditions.

    50 MP Eye AF Group Selfie Camera

    More people, more smiles, no worries. A larger FOV. helps you capture those larger-than-life moments, with more people and scenery in it. V40e series comes with a 50 MP front camera which is the highest pixel count for a front camera in its segment that allows you to capture clear & crisp photos with the front camera. The 92° Wide-Angle Field-of-View allows you to capture more people in the frame without hassle.

    2x Professional Portrait Mode

    Steal the spotlight with the 2x Professional Portrait Mode that sharpens facial clarity and texture, while the natural bokeh effect ensures you stand out in every frame.

    Ultra-Stable 4K Video

    Hybrid Image Stabilization (OlS+EIS) in the rear camera enables smooth 4K video shooting by eliminating the effect of unwanted handshakes. 4K resolution offers greater flexibility for post-production editing. Let the world experience your very own director's cut! The front camera also supports 4K video shooting.

    Studio-Quality AURA LIGHT Portrait

    V40e empowers you to capture stunning photos in every memorable moment. Be it in warm or cool light settings, it seamlessly blends you into your surroundings, minimizing light from ambient sources and harmonizing colors with the environment. With Studio Quality Aura Light, V40e ensures accurate facial details even under difficult lighting conditions.

    AI Eraser

    Erase unwanted elements and perfect your images with ease for flawless photos.

    AI Photo Enhancer

    Revive your beautiful moments with our AI Photo Enhancer feature, improving clarity, optimizing color performance, and reducing noise with just a single tap.

    MediaTek Dimensity 7300

    MediaTek Dimensity 7300 further reduces power consumption while enhancing real-time focus speed and image quality optimization for high-quality imaging.

    Closed-box Dual Stereo Speaker

    Level up your sonic experience with the dual speakers and a powerful 300% max volume. Smartphone sound just got real like never before.

    IP64 Dust and Water Resistance

    V40e is equipped with IP64 Dust and Water Resistance, which effectively prevents dust and water splashes from damaging your phone. The newly upgraded Wet Touch Technology algorithm allows you to use the phone even when your hands are wet.
