    Product Description
    Immersive 9.24 cm (3.64) Big Cover Display with Gorilla Victus 2 Protection

    The Zero Flip features a 3.64 AMOLED Cover Display with a 120 Hz refresh rate, ensuring fluid interactions. The display offers excellent visibility with up to 1400nits brightness and is robustly protected by Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2, enhancing durability.

    Interactive and Intuitive Cover Display

    The cover display supports top apps directly, allowing seamless interaction without unfolding the phone. It offers smart cards for quick access to tools and customizable options like Cute Pets, live wallpapers, and an Always-On Display (AOD), providing both functionality and personalization.

    Premium & Durable Design

    The Zero Flip offers ultimate portability with its 7.64 mm slim design when unfolded. The foldable form factor features a Zero Gap Hinge with minimal crease and is tested for 400,000 folds, ensuring durability. The innovative hinge allows Flex Mode with multi-angle hovering from 30° to 150°, enhancing usability.

    50MP 4K Selfie Vlog Camera

    The Infinix Zero Flip comes up to you as your complete camera solution, whether you are a traveller shooting vlogs or lifestyle influencers or someone who just wants the perfect shot, Zero Flip is here to rescue you with a 50 MP Front Camera with a front flash which supports 4K@60 FPS ultra-clear videos ensuring every frame is captured perfectly. With The Go Pro Mode, you can enjoy the capabilities of Go Pro easily with the Zero Flip. Not just these things, it also comes with plenty of amazing modes & crazy stuff like hover selfies, dual preview, 4K Vlogs, etc, and much more.

    50MP OIS + 50MP Ultra-wide | Works with GoPro

    Capture every moment in stunning detail with the 50MP primary rear camera with Optical Image Stabilization (OIS). The second 50MP ultra-wide lens adds versatility, while the GoPro Mode allows seamless action shots. Record lifelike 4K@30FPS videos, perfect for vlogs and social media content. With Dual Rear Flash and plenty of modes, Go Pro Mode, 4K Vlogs, Dual Video, Sky Shop, and a lot more for your every need

    Minimal Crease with Maximum Fun | 17.52 cm (6.9) FHD+ LTPO AMOLED Display

    Experience true cinematic immersion on the 17.52 cm (6.9) FHD+ AMOLED LTPO display and it supports 100% DCI-P3 color accuracy to give you a vivid and rich color in visuals. With a smooth 120Hz refresh rate, enjoy vibrant visuals whether you're gaming or binge-watching.

    Biggest Battery with 70 W Fast Charge

    Say goodbye to frequent charging and hello to a seamless experience with Infinix Zero Flip which brings the Biggest 4720 mAh Battery & 70 W Fastest Type C-Charging. Whether you're navigating work tasks, streaming your favourite shows, or capturing life's moments it delivers the reliability and performance you need. Elevate your experience with power that lasts longer & keeps going On & On!!

    Infinix AI

    Stay ahead with the latest AI features. The Zero Flip comes loaded with AI features like AI Eraser, AI Charge Protection AI Gallery, AI Wallpaper Generator, and Smart Suggestions to make your experience seamless. Folax powered by Gemini, the AI Voice Assistant is always ready to answer queries, make suggestions, and help with everyday tasks.

    Unmatched Performance

    Unlock unparalleled performance with the Powerful MediaTek Dimensity 8020 octa-core processor, Now Unleash the power of limitless possibilities & experience a perfect balance of power and efficiency, making it ideal for demanding tasks like gaming, content creation, and more with advanced AI capabilities to make your life at ease.

    Do More & Store More!

    With up to 16 GB RAM (8 GB RAM + 8 GB Virtual RAM) and massive 512 GB UFS 3.1 storage, you can easily store, transfer files, and optimize your storage for peak performance without any stress. Whether you're a digital hoarder, a content creator, or simply someone who wants to keep their digital life organized, Zero Flip's big storage has got you covered.

    Feel Every beat with JBL sound

    Immerse yourself in rich, stereo sound that elevates your music, videos, and gaming to a whole new level with Infinix Zero Flip which has collaborated with JBL to optimize and improve sound quality. Whether you're enjoying a quiet evening at home or hosting a lively get-together, the dual speakers deliver crystal-clear, balanced audio to elevate your audio experience

    XOS 14.5 powered by Android 14

    The latest XOS 14.5 runs on Android 14 and features improved AI integration and enhanced customization options. Enjoy smoother interactions, advanced security features, and a more intuitive user interface. XOS 14.5 also brings better app management and system optimization, ensuring a seamless and efficient user experience.

    Convenience At Go!!

    With the Infinix Zero Flip, convenience is on the Go with plenty of features and the integration of advanced AI! With NFC support, you don't need to carry physical cards and can easily store cards safely in your Zero Flip. With Folax-Powered by Gemini, Advanced AI is here to resolve your every need in seconds.
