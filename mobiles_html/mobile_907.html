    • Description
    The 200 is a state-of-the-art smartphone designed to cater to the needs of tech-savvy users. It features a large 6.7-inch AMOLED display with high-definition resolution and True 1.07 billion colors for a visually stunning experience. The advanced triple rear camera system allows users to capture every detail with clarity, whether shooting expansive landscapes or intricate macro shots. Under the hood, the 200 is powered by a robust 3rd generation Snapdragon 7 processor, offering lag-free performance even during intensive use. The device is equipped with a 5200mAh 2nd Gen Silicon Carbon battery supported 100W Super Fast Charge, providing long-lasting battery life and rapid charging. Additionally, the 200 runs on AI-Powered MagicOS 8.0, which introduces a host of smart features and enhancements, making everyday tasks smoother and more interactive. With its sleek design the 200 not only performs excellently but also looks stylish in any setting.
