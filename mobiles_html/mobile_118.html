    Product Description
    MediaTek Dimensity 7025 Processor

    Unleash the ultimate performance with the MediaTek Dimensity 7025 octa-core processor. The powerful octa-core processor offers an unbelievably smooth experience while gaming, multi-tasking, or consuming content. MediaTek Dimensity 7025 is based on 2.5GHz frequencies that bring you Superfast 5G performance, an unparalleled camera experience, efficient video processing, and superior gaming capabilities. Its advanced 5G technology ensures faster downloads and better power efficiency, making it the perfect choice for those who demand the best.

    6000 mAh Battery + 33 W Charger

    The moto g64 5G, powered by a leading 6000mAh battery, promises endless entertainment on the go. Dive into the fastest 5G world without the fear of running out of power. Its robust 6000mAh battery supports all-day use to work and play as much as you want without worrying about running out of juice. This battery is designed to last you all day today and well into tomorrow. With TurboPower 33W charger, you can get hours of power in just minutes, so you can recharge your phone quickly and easily.

    12 GB + 256 GB 5G Smartphone

    Experience smooth performance with the 8 GB RAM + 128 GB storage smartphone. Effortlessly navigate through your apps on the moto g64 5G that's powered by an impressive in-built 12 GB of memory. Say goodbye to storage woes because, with 128 GB internal storage, you have ample space for photos, movies, songs, apps, and games. This phone enables smooth multitasking and provides ample space for all your multimedia content with LPDDR4X memory type and UFS 2.2 storage. Whether it's gaming, binge-watching, expansive playlists, essential apps, or capturing moments the moto g64 5G can handle it all effortlessly. And if you need more space, you can expand the storage further with a microSD card of up to 1 TB.

    Shake-free 50 MP OIS Camera with Quad Pixel Technology

    Capture the beauty of the world with the advanced Shake Free 50MP camera, equipped with OIS for ultra-stable pictures and videos, accompanied by Quad Pixel technology for stunning low-light photos and incredibly smooth videos. This camera offers three different experiences in one advanced 8MP autofocus camera that combines ultra-wide shots, brilliant close-ups, and depth sensors. The OIS compensates for any unwanted camera movements, ensuring clarity even in low light, while the 50MP sensor and Quad Pixel technology provide 4x better low-light sensitivity for sharper and more vibrant photos day or night. With the 16MP selfie camera, capture Insta-worthy content for you.

    Fast 5G Speed

    Experience the power of 5G like never before with our phone's impressive 14 5G Bands, boasting 4x4 MIMO and improved 3 carrier aggregation. Unleash your favorite content and activities at unprecedented 5G speeds, ensuring seamless connectivity and extraordinary performance.

    120 Hz 16.5 cm (6.5) FHD+ IPS LCD Display

    Immerse yourself in unparalleled visual excellence with a dynamic 120 Hz, 16.51 cm (6.5) Full HD+ IPS LCD display. Experience smoothness and fluidity with an impressive 120Hz refresh rate and 240Hz touch sampling. This ensures that every tap, swipe, and scroll is incredibly responsive and displayed in high definition with unparalleled fluidity, whether you're transitioning between apps, gaming, or browsing the web, your favourite content will come to life like never before.

    Premium, Colourful and Stylish Design

    The moto g64 5G is a phone that's designed to stand out and make a statement with its sleek look and delightful tactile feel. Its streamlined aluminium camera housing comes with an Acrylic Glass finish (3D Premium PMMA). It's thin (8.89 mm) and lightweight (192 g), making it one of the lightest phones with a 6000mAh battery.

    Stereo Speakers with Dolby Atmos

    Step into a world of extraordinary sound quality. Treat your ears to premium audio output with Dolby Atmos Dual Stereo Speakers and Moto Spatial Sound technologies. Feel the depth of enhanced bass, the clarity of vocals, and the immersive spatial effects, even at maximum volume. Whether you're using headphones or not, experience a sense of depth, spaciousness, and crystal-clear sound that's tailored just for you.

    Android 14

    Customize your smartphone with your personal touch with the Latest Android 14 out of the box. Change the colour schemes, themes, and languages of your apps and get complete control over which apps can access your data. Additionally, you will receive guaranteed Android15 operating system updates and 3 years of security updates. Stay ahead with privacy updates that boost your health, safety, and data security, along with expanded accessibility features.

    IP52 Water-repellent Design

    The moto g64 5G offers exceptional protection from raindrops, splashes, and spills. Designed to ensure your journey remains uninterrupted, the moto g64 5G stands ready to accompany you through any adventure. Whether you're jogging through the park or engaged in an important call amidst a drizzle, our IP52 water-repellent design gives you the confidence to navigate any situation worry-free.
