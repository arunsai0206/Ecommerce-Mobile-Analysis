    • Description
    The Motorola Razr 50 Ultra Beach Sand 256GB with 8GB RAM is a premium foldable smartphone designed to combine cutting-edge technology with a sleek, modern design. It represents a perfect blend of innovation, style, and powerful performance, offering a unique foldable experience. Key Features and Specifications: Design and Display: Foldable Design: The Razr 50 Ultra has a 6.9-inch pOLED display when unfolded, offering an immersive, edge-to-edge viewing experience. It boasts a Full HD+ resolution (2640 x 1080 pixels), delivering vibrant colors and sharp details. Compact Folded Form: When folded, it becomes a pocket-friendly device with a 3.6-inch outer display, which allows you to check notifications, respond to messages, and control music without opening the phone. Beach Sand Finish: The phone comes in a stylish Beach Sand color, adding a touch of elegance and sophistication to its design. Performance: Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1 Chipset: Equipped with one of the most powerful processors, the Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1, the phone provides excellent performance for multitasking, gaming, and heavy app usage. It has a 3.2 GHz primary core for handling demanding tasks. 8GB RAM: With 8GB of RAM, the device offers smooth multitasking, allowing you to switch between apps effortlessly without lag or delay. 256GB Storage: The phone provides ample space for storing apps, photos, videos, and files, ensuring you have plenty of room for all your digital needs. Camera System: 50 MP Primary Camera: The rear camera features a 50 MP sensor with an f/1.8 aperture, capturing detailed and vibrant photos in various lighting conditions. 13 MP Ultra-Wide Camera: It also includes a 13 MP ultra-wide lens, perfect for capturing expansive landscapes or large group shots with ease. Selfie Camera: The inner folding display doubles as a selfie viewfinder, providing a high-quality 32 MP front-facing camera for crisp and clear selfies. Battery and Charging: 3,800 mAh Battery: The Razr 50 Ultra is equipped with a 3,800 mAh battery, designed to last through a full day of use with moderate to heavy usage. Fast Charging: It supports fast charging to quickly recharge the battery, so you're not left waiting for long to power up. Software and Features: Android 13: The device runs on Android 13, offering a clean, user-friendly interface and access to the latest features and security updates. Foldable UX: The foldable design allows you to make the most out of the large screen for productivity, entertainment, and multitasking. The device provides intuitive multitasking features, such as split-screen apps and flexible screen layouts.
