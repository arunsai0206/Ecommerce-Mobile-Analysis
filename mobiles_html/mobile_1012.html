    Product Description
    Powerful 5000 mAh Battery

    With a powerful 5000 mAh battery and a Type-C charger, this Motorola E7 Power smartphone helps you breeze through your daily workloads with ease. You can play music for up to 76 hours, browse the web for up to 12 hours, or stream videos for up to 14 hours on a single full charge.

    Large RAM and Internal Storage Capacity

    This smartphone’s 4 GB of RAM coupled with the octa-core processor enables smooth performance and instant feedback with every swipe, touch, and tap. Plus, the 64 GB inbuilt storage lets you save your music, images, movies, and more with ease. In case you need more storage, you can expand this memory by up to 1 TB using a microSD card.

    16.51 cm (6.5) Max Vision HD+ Display

    The immersive 16.51 cm (6.5) Max Vision HD+ display of this smartphone enhances your movie- and video-watching as well as your gaming experience with rich and vivid details. Its 20:9 aspect ratio lets you enjoy more of your favourite content on its wide display.

    Fingerprint Sensor

    You can safely unlock this smartphone with just a touch by using the fingerprint sensor that's located within the 'batwing' logo on the back.

    Stock Android Experience

    This smartphone features a near-stock version of Android, enabling you to enjoy a bloat-free Android experience.

    13 MP Rear Dual Camera System

    While the 13 MP dual-camera system on the rear, with fast-focus, allows you to shoot beautiful portraits, landscapes, events, and more with great clarity, the 2 MP macro camera lets you capture minute details of a subject/object for a sharply defined image.

    Water Resistance

    The IP52-rated water-repellent design protects this phone from damage even if you get caught in a drizzle or accidentally spill a drink on it.

    Google Assistant Support

    You can press the dedicated button that's located on the side of this smartphone to launch Google Assistant. Thanks to Google Assistant, you can not only get answers to your queries by using voice commands but also get weather reports, traffic updates, and more without having to type, tap, or swipe on your screen.

    Stunning Design

    Featuring a stylish and attractive design, this smartphone is sure to grab the attention of onlookers whenever you take it out.
