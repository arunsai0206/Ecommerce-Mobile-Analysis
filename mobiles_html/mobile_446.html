    Product Description
    High-quality Camera

    The smartphone's 50MP and 2MP rear cameras capture sharp and vibrant pictures. The 50MP primary camera captures intricate details, while the secondary lens adds depth for professional-quality shots. The 8MP front camera lets you capture clear selfies. Additionally, the smartphone's camera supports multiple modes, including night, portrait, pro, dual video, and more, letting you adjust the settings as per the requirements.

    Long Battery Life

    The powerful battery of the smartphone will ensure uninterrupted performance throughout the day. The battery capacity is 6000mAh, which ensures that the phone will last through your busy schedule, be it for work or just binge-watching. Additionally, it supposes 44w charging, ensuring you do not need to wait long to use it.

    Bright and Smooth Display

    The 17.07 cm LCD display ensures visibility and clarity. With a 2408 x 1080 Pixel resolution and 120 Hz refresh rate, every scroll, swipe, and video will look smooth and vibrant. The 83 per cent NTSC colour saturation ensures bright colours, and LED light-emitting material enhances the brightness for improved visibility in all lighting conditions.

    Premium Look

    The smartphone has a durable and premium design. Its composite plastic back cover ensures resilience and gives it a sleek and modern finish. With an IP64 rating, it is resistant to dust and splashes. The side-mounted fingerprint sensor ensures convenient use and gives the smartphone a luxurious feel. It is additionally designed to be lightweight, which provides ease of use.

    Optimum Storage Space

    The smartphone's storage capacity is 128 GB, which allows you to install many games, files, and pictures. It is expandable up to 1TB, which allows you to get extra storage for all of the important stuff. Additionally, the smartphone's 8GB RAM ensures smooth functioning. There will be no lags even if you are using multiple apps at a time.
