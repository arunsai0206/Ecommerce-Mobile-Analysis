Capture and keep memories with virtually limitless capacity.

    Experience the next-level 5G performance and with the Snapdragon 6s Gen 3 chipset.     The 5000 mAh capacity ensures your device keeps up with your busy lifestyle, providing ample power for all-day use and then some. It's the perfect combination for those who demand both endurance and speed.
