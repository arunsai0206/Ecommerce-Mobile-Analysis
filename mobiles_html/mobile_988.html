    Product Description
    Narrow Bezel

    The days of having your vision obscured by a big bezel are long gone. The Redmi Note 13 5G immerses you in every moment, whether you're streaming your favourite programme or conquering a new mobile game, thanks to its amazing 93.3% screen-to-body ratio. Envision being engrossed in the activity, with nothing to divert your attention from the striking spectacle in front of you.

    AMOLED Display

    The captivating images on the Redmi Note 13 5G's 16.94 cm (6.67) AMOLED display will enthral you. The very small bezel guarantee that every scene is rendered with unmatched depth and clarity. The AMOLED technology provides an absolutely amazing experience, whether you're exploring a virtual world or binge-watching your favourite series. Additionally, you can navigate precisely even with moist fingertips from an unexpected downpour or a refreshing drink thanks to optimised touch recognition.

    Seamless Visuals

    The ultra-smooth 120 Hz display of the Redmi Note 13 5G combines elegance with smoothness. The high refresh rate ensures that every swipe, scroll, and press is silky smooth. Say goodbye to jerky animations and sluggish transitions. The 120 Hz display makes sure that every interaction is smooth and seamless, whether you're playing intense games or multitasking like a pro.

    Sleek Design

    With its newfound slimness, reduced weight, and elegant design, the Redmi Note 13 5G is a real featherweight champion. Its sleek profile and ergonomic design defy expectations, weighing only 173.5 g and measuring just 7.6 mm thin. Whether you hold it in your hand or slip it into your pocket, you'll be grateful for its smoothness every time you reach for it.

    Corning Gorilla Glass 5

    Although accidents may happen, the display of your Redmi Note 13 5G is always safe with Corning Gorilla Glass 5. Bid farewell to the unsightly cracks and scratches - this very resilient glass is designed to endure the stresses of regular usage, giving you peace of mind that your screen is secure.

    Super 108 MP Camera

    With the Redmi Note 13 5G's 108 MP triple camera configuration, you can capture every moment in breathtaking detail. Every picture, whether it's a candid portrait or an amazing scenery, has the potential to be a masterpiece. Furthermore, you can zoom in without sacrificing clarity thanks to the 3X in-sensor zoom, guaranteeing that every detail is captured for future generations.

    Blazing-fast 5G Performance

    With the lightning-fast speed of the Redmi Note 13 5G, discover the power of 5G. Unmatched speed and responsiveness are provided by the MediaTek Dimensity 6080 CPU, whether you're streaming, gaming, or multitasking. Additionally, you can make use of all the advantages of 5G without compromising battery life thanks to MediaTek 5G UltraSave technology. This puts the best of both worlds right at your fingertips.

    Powerful Battery

    With its 5000 mAh battery and 33 W rapid charging, the Redmi Note 13 5G combines power and ease. You can quickly replenish your battery with rapid charging and resume your favourite activities. Additionally, you won't ever have to worry about running out of power because of the enormous 5000 mAh capacity.
