    Product Description
    360° Damage-proof Armour Body

    The 360° Damage-proof Armour Body of the Oppo A3 Pro 5G offers unparalleled durability. This smartphone's unique screen glass is less likely to pierce since it is made to resist drops, water, dust, and even heat shocks. You can rely on your phone to function and be undamaged even if it falls unexpectedly or gets caught in a downpour.

    45 W SUPERVOOC Flash Charge

    Time is critical in the fast-paced society we live in today. With the 45 W SUPERVOOC Flash Charge technology of the Oppo A3 Pro 5G, you can charge it to 50% capacity in just 30 minutes. With your quick charging capabilities, you can plug in and go without any delays.

    5100 mAh Hyper-energy Battery

    Powered by the potent 5100 mAh Hyper-energy Battery, you can conquer your day. This battery, which is housed inside the stylish phone body, is made to last "like new" for more than four years, giving you dependable power for all of your activities. Its performance, lifespan, and safety are all improved by cutting-edge innovations, which make it an absolute powerhouse.

    120 Hz Ultra Bright Display

    Experience a feast for the eyes with the 120 Hz Ultra Bright Display. Even in harsh sunshine, this Amazon-certified screen produces amazing brightness without the need for any adjustments. With Eye Comfort Mode to preserve your eyesight when using the screen for extended periods of time, the great screen-to-body ratio makes for an immersive viewing experience.

    Splash Touch

    The Oppo A3 Pro 5G maintains its excellent responsiveness even when your drink splashes over the screen or you have damp hands. No matter how wet the weather gets, your phone will always work perfectly owing to Splash Touch technology. Ideal for people whose busy lifestyles require their gadgets to keep up with.

    Premium Gleaming Design

    Make a statement with the Premium Gleaming Design of the Oppo A3 Pro 5G. The phone has an upscale appearance thanks to its elegant metallic centre bezel and slender 7.68 mm frame. It combines functionality with style by being thin and weighing only 186g even though it has a sizable battery.

    Shine Bright, Stay Slim

    For the Oppo A3 Pro 5G, stability is everything. Its compact and thin form doesn't sacrifice battery capacity; it has a sizable 5100 mAh battery housed in a thin housing. This guarantees that you appear chic and smart while being energised throughout the day.

    AI LinkBoost

    Leveraging AI LinkBoost, stay connected with ease. With the help of this feature, network switching will be seamless and there will be faster signal recovery, more stable signals, and optimised congestion. You may benefit from quicker sharing, more accurate navigation, and a stable connection for all of your online activities, regardless of the network circumstances.

    AI Eraser

    AI Eraser makes photo correction simple. With just a single press, you may use this tool to eliminate undesired objects from your photos. The AI Eraser ensures that your photos are always flawless by effortlessly replacing distracting elements, such as bystanders, with a realistic backdrop.

    Ultra Volume Mode

    With Ultra Volume Mode, you'll never miss another call. With the use of this innovative technology, you may increase the volume by up to 300% to improve your hearing in busy surroundings. Your talks stay clear and concise whether you're in a busy event or a busy metropolis.

    MediaTek Dimensity 6300 5G

    Take advantage of the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 5G chipset's outstanding performance. This platform, which was constructed using a 6 nm technique, guarantees minimal power consumption without sacrificing performance. Experience the advantages of a 5G connection without using up all of your battery life, resulting in seamless and effective interactions.

    RAM Expansion

    RAM Expansion makes multitasking easy to manage. The phone borrows ROM (storage memory) and turns it into RAM (running memory) when needed thanks to this function. The Oppo A3 Pro 5G maintains its fluidity and functionality even under high load, guaranteeing a flawless user experience.

    50 MP AI Main Camera

    Use the 50MP AI Main Camera to take breathtaking pictures. This camera produces very clear, incredibly lifelike, and incredibly detailed images by utilising cutting-edge hardware and clever AI algorithms. Every photo has been altered to bring your memories to life.

    Portrait Mode

    Use the Oppo A3 Pro 5G's Portrait Mode to take visually stunning photographs. Together, the two cameras provide clear portraits of humans against beautiful bokeh backgrounds. With just your smartphone, you can take portrait photos of a professional calibre, turning each one into a work of art.

    AI Portrait Retouching

    AI Portrait Retouching can help you show off your genuine attractiveness. Hypersensitive retouching is provided by this function, resulting in naturally stunning photos. You may fine-tune your appearance with 10 retouch options on the front camera and a sliding retouch scale on the back camera.

    Trinity Engine

    Experience the Trinity Engine's unmatched battery performance, stability, and fluidity. This exclusive to OPPO system-level processing engine improves app load times increases storage through app compression and offers an amazing user experience all around.

    50-month Fluency Protection

    The 50-month Fluency Protection on your phone will keep it feeling brand-new for years. Extensive laboratory testing demonstrates that, after 50 months of regular usage, the Oppo A3 Pro 5G ages by less than 10%, guaranteeing durability and dependability.

    Unboxing Surprise

    With the Anti-drop Shield Case included, open with enthusiasm. This premium-quality, unique high-value case gives your already sturdy Oppo A3 Pro 5G an additional layer of toughness and outstanding protection.
