    Product Description
    Clean UI with Minimal Pre-installed Apps

    Imagine a smartphone experience free from clutter, where every swipe and tap feels effortless. With the Note 40 Pro+ 5G, you get just that – a clean user interface devoid of unnecessary apps. Say goodbye to bloatware and hello to a streamlined digital experience.

    Active Halo AI Lighting

    Meet your new sidekick, Active Halo. Powered by cutting-edge AI technology, Active Halo adds a touch of magic to your everyday interactions. Whether it's lighting up your notifications or enhancing your gaming experience, Active Halo is there to brighten up your day.

    Truly Immersive 3D Curved 120 Hz AMOLED Display

    Immerse yourself in a world of vivid colors and stunning visuals with the Note 40 Pro+ 5G's 3D curved 120 Hz AMOLED display. With ultra-narrow bezels and Corning Gorilla Glass protection, every glance at your phone feels like stepping into a high-definition dream.

    Bypass Charging

    To minimize heat buildup and protect the battery life during heavy usage like gaming or watching videos, The NOTE 40 Pro+ 5G can bypass the battery and directly charge the motherboard to reduce heat buildup by 4.5°C significantly.

    Wireless Reverse Charging

    Never run out of battery again with the Note 40 Pro+ 5G's wireless reverse charging feature. Simply flip the switch and power up your other devices on the go.

    Power Up your Mobile Experience

    With multi-speed fast charging and a high-energy-density 4600 mAh battery, the Note 40 Pro+ 5G ensures that you're always ready for whatever the day throws at you. And with robust safety features, you can charge with confidence, knowing that your device is protected.

    Infinix X1 Cheetah Chip

    Experience unparalleled performance with the Infinix X1 Cheetah Chip, a revolutionary innovation that takes charging management to the next level. With high-precision power detection and versatile scene support, the X1 Cheetah Chip ensures that your device stays powered up and secure at all times.

    Dual JBL Speakers

    Immerse yourself in crystal-clear sound with the Note 40 Pro+ 5G's dual JBL stereo speakers. Whether you're watching movies, listening to music, or playing games, the Note 40 Pro+ 5G delivers an audio experience like no other.

    Powerful Processor

    Powered by a 6 nm MediaTek Dimensity 7020 5G processor, the Note 40 Pro+ 5G offers unparalleled performance and efficiency. From gaming to multitasking, this processor can handle it all with ease.

    Massive Storage

    With up to 24 GB of RAM and 256 GB of ROM, the Note 40 Pro+ 5G offers ample storage for all your apps, photos, and media. Say goodbye to storage woes and hello to seamless performance.

    Stunning Camera

    Capture life's most precious moments in breathtaking detail with the Note 40 Pro+ 5G's 108MP OIS triple camera setup. Whether you're a professional photographer or just love snapping photos, the Note 40 Pro+ 5G delivers stunning results every time.

    Blazing-fast Charging

    With 100 W multi-speed fast charging, the Note 40 Pro+ 5G ensures that you spend less time plugged in and more time on the go. And thanks to its 4600 mAh high-energy-density battery, you can enjoy all-day usage on a single charge, no matter how demanding your day gets.
