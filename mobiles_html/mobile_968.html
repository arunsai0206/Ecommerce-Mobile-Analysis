    Product Description
    Stylish Design

    The stylish designs featured in this smartphone makes it attractive. You can choose from its two different finishes, a diamond pattern glass finish and leather-like finish according to your preference.

    Easy Multitasking

    This smartphone uses the Infinix MemFusion technology which doubles 8 GB of RAM to 16 GB and enables you to multitask easilywhile running multiple apps in the background.

    Enhanced Storage

    This smartphone has an internal storage capacity of 128 GB which can be extended by up to 1 TB with a microSD slot so that you have enough space to store all your files.

    AI-powered Dual Camera

    You can capture mesmerising photos with this smartphone’s 50 MP dual camera. The dual LED flash allows you to click photographs even in low light. Its 5 MP front camera allows you to take attractive selfies. It has over 10 AI-powered camera modes that enable you to capture creative photos.

    Large Display

    This smartphone has a large 16.7 cm (6.6) HD+ display for immersive viewing. It has a refresh rate of about 90 Hz that enables smooth visuals. Its 180 Hz touch sampling rate provides fast response.

    Powerful Processor

    This smartphone features a MediaTek G37 processor that has an octa-core CPU and an Arm Mali-G57 GPU for fast and smooth processing.

    Assured Safety

    The Infinix HOT 30i features a fingerprint scanner and face recognition unlock feature that ensures maximum safety and keeps your data secure.

    Triple Card Slot

    This smartphone has 2 slots that support 4G SIM cards and a slot for microSD card for convenience.

    XOS 12

    The Infinix HOT 30i uses XOS 12 UI that provides the latest updates so that your smartphone always stays updated and new. It has several other additional features like Video Assistant, Game Mode, Social Turbo, Eye Care, AI Gallery, OTG Support, and much more.
