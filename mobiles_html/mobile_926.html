    Product Description
    Extra Powerful

    The secret behind Phone (2a) Plus’ power is the MediaTek Dimensity 7350 Pro 5G processor, exclusive to Nothing. Featuring cutting-edge TSMC 4 nm Gen 2 technology, with 8 cores running up to 3.0 GHz – nearly 10% faster than Phone (2a) – everyday multi-tasking is now a walk in the park. It’s supported by an ARM Mali-G610 MC4 GPU clocking at 1.3 GHz, which is 30% faster than the Phone (2a). These components, along with Nothing OS, give an Antutu score that surpasses 805,000 allowing Phone (2a) Plus to blast through tasks with speed and efficiency in order to keep up with the busiest of lifestyles.

    Extra Pixels

    Take your photography to the next level with Phone (2a) Plus’ triple 50 MP camera system. Boasting a large light-absorbing 50 MP sensor with an f/1.88 lens, an ultrawide 50 MP sensor with a 114º field of view, and a 50 MP front camera for superstar selfies. All three sensors support direct 50 MP photo output, HDR photo capture, and 4K video recording. Additional features include Motion Capture for blur-free action shots, Night Mode for enhanced low-light photography, and Portrait Optimiser for the ultimate group shots.

    Extra Colourful

    Phone (2a) Plus has a flexible FHD+ 17.01 cm (6.7) AMOLED display that can deliver stunningly accurate colour reproduction of 1.07 billion colours. Boasting a peak brightness of 1300 nits, it ensures superb visibility, even when the sun is shining at its brightest. Interactions are effortlessly smooth thanks to the 120 Hz adaptive refresh rate, seamlessly adjusting to content and a 240 Hz touch sampling rate for instant responsiveness. To protect against eye strain, Phone (2a) Plus has pulse-width modulation (PWM) dimming at 2160 Hz. All in all, it contributes to a beautifully efficient and fluid display that can keep up with the most demanding lifestyles.

    Extra Unique

    Phone (2a) Plus introduces two new colours for Nothing smartphones: metallic Grey and Black. Reminiscent of machinery, the metallic finish offers a new premium expression of Nothing’s design language, while highlighting the cutting-edge upgrades of Phone (2a) Plus. Achieving this look involved intricate texturing, over 20 manufacturing methods, and extensive sampling to perfect the final effect. Using innovative nano-coating, multi-layer printing, and proprietary ink formulations, each layer within the phone was meticulously applied to achieve a distinctive metallic look and colour.

    Extra (special) Android

    Welcome to Nothing’s world. Explore an entirely unique Android 14 experience with Nothing OS 2.6, the latest software development from the team at Nothing. Explore the versatile Nothing Widget library for instant access to your most-used apps and functions directly from your lock and home screens. Dive into a whole host of functional customisation options to create a personal smartphone experience that eliminates distractions and bloatware, increasing productivity. Ask, listen, and learn from ChatGPT on the go. Get the answers you need faster and at your fingertips. Text, voice, or image search right from your home screen. Nothing Phone (2a) Plus comes with 3 years of major Android updates and 4 years of security updates.

    Extra Robust

    Phone (2a) Plus has undergone a rigorous suite of test cases in order to ensure uncompromised quality and longevity. In total, we run more than 95,000 individual tests for 68 different quality control protocols. And it's not just hardware. To ensure our software is continually operating and new, we run an extensive testing period pre-launch in collaboration with our global Nothing community.
