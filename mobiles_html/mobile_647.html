    ZEISS Professional Portrait Camera

Imagine capturing a candid moment, where a single click transforms it into a professional-grade portrait, achieving new heights in mobile photography.

No more strategic positioning; capture the essence of your group effortlessly. Imagine a family reunion where everyone, from elders to kids, looks picture-perfect without the chaos of placement.

    The V30 Pro houses the MediaTek Dimensity 8200 5G Processor, crafted with TSMC's industry-leading 4 nm process. With a 3.1 GHz super-large core, this powerhouse offers enhanced performance and power efficiency. Picture seamless navigation, high-quality streaming, and gaming without compromise – the V30 Pro sets the stage for a smooth and responsive user experience.

    The V30 Pro boasts the largest battery in the V series, a staggering 5000mAh. While retaining a slim profile, it supports 80 W FlashCharge. Imagine a device that keeps pace with your dynamic lifestyle – the V30 Pro delivers ultra-long standby times, keeping you connected without the anxiety of battery life. From dawn till dusk, experience endurance redefined.

    The front camera isn't just for solo selfies; it's a powerhouse for group shots.
