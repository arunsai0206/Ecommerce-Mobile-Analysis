    Premium and Elegant Design

    Experience the perfect blend of style and functionality with the SMART 9HD which brings the refreshed design with new Multilayer Glass Finish Back which is a Smudge-free blend the perfect combination of style, elegance with durability. It has flat edges with colour-matched frames. The camera module has a new sleek & integrated look.

    Built To Last!

    The SMART 9HD is built to withstand the rigors of your daily adventures. With 2,50,000+ Drop tests & 200+ Quality and Durability tests, it assures the toughness of the phone. The phone has passed the 6-sided drop protection test which enables your device to be ready to face your everyday hindrances. With IP54 Ratings Smart 9HD is splash & dust proof. The Smart 9HD has been tested for extreme temperature storage test.

    Elevate your experience today and see the difference with Big a 6.7HD+ Punch-Hole Display with 90Hz Refresh Rate. Designed for the ultimate viewing pleasure, our display offers a seamless blend with unparalleled brightness of up to 500nits making every moment extraordinary.

    Experience sound like never before with SMART 9HD dual speakers, enhanced by DTS sound processing technology. Whether you're watching movies, playing games, or streaming your favorite music, enjoy rich, immersive audio. Elevate every moment with audio that’s as dynamic and captivating as your visuals.

    Clear Camera with Zoom Flash

    Turn everyday moments into extraordinary memories with SMART 9HD which has 13MP Dual Rear Camera with Zoom Flash. It lets you capture sharp, vibrant photos which is designed to bring out the best in every shot. The Camera is capable of recognizing scenes and conditions, to give you the best results every time you click a picture. With plenty of modes such as Portrait mode, AR Mode, Time Lapse, etc. which fit every need of yours. With a Selfie Camera of 8MP with LED Flash, take clearer selfies & with wide angle support your group selfies are now more enhanced & better.

    Interactive Dyanamic Bar

    Infinix SMART 9HD brings to you the Interactive Dynamic Bar which comes up with loaded features such as Face Unlock, Background Call, Charging Animation, Charge completion reminder, and Low Battery Reminder.

    Store More & More!!

    With up to 6 GB RAM(3 GB+3 GB Virtual) and Big 64 GB Storage, you can easily store, transfer files, and optimize your storage for peak performance without any stress. Whether you're a heavy social media user, a content creator, or simply storing photos, videos, etc. SMART 9HD got you covered.

    Big Battery!!

    Say goodbye to frequent charging and hello to a seamless experience with Smart 9HD which brings a 5000 mAh battery with Type C-Charging. Whether you're navigating work tasks, streaming your favourite shows, or capturing life's moments it delivers the reliability and performance you need. Elevate your experience with power that lasts longer & keeps going On & On!!

    Octa-core Processor

    Experience the fast speed and seamless multitasking with the Mediatek Helio G50 processor. Built for efficiency and power, it handles everything from gaming and streaming to productivity and creativity with ease. Enjoy smoother app launches, faster downloads, and lag-free performance, even during intense tasks.

    Loaded with Features

    Get the latest XOS 14 based on the Android 14 Go with a bunch of cool additions like DTS speaker, Eye Care, AI Gallery, OTG Support, and many more.
