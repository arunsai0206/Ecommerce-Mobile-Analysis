    Powerful Performance

    T3x 5G is equipped with the Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 - 4 nm 5G mobile platform and features a 8-core CPU architecture. The next-gen 4 nm platform boasts a benchmark score of approximately 560K+, offering the most powerful performance in its price range and providing ultra-smooth gaming and multitasking.

    Massive Battery

    The ultra-long lasting 6000 mAh battery life offers you a non-stop immersive experience whether you're gaming, binge-watching, or listening to music without worrying about the battery running out.

    Dual Stereo Speaker

    The 300% volume and Audio Booster, combined with a Dual Stereo Speaker, bring you a highly impactful cinematic auditory feast. The sound quality is finer and clearer for a more dimensional and robust effect. Immerse yourself in the atmosphere and emotions and experience even the most nuanced movements and emotional shifts of each character in films.

    120 Hz High-brightness FHD+ Display

    T3x features a 120 Hz FHD+ high-brightness punch-hole display. A large display delivers a cinematic and immersive experience. Smooth playback, a wide field of view, rich, vivid, and high-contrast colors, as well as clear light and dark details, jointly bring you an immersive viewing experience.

    Vibrant Design

    Exquisite and high-end dial design: The camera adopts an exquisite and sophisticated "premium dial" design. Paired with precision gears, the eye-catching design showcases fine lines and high-precision craftsmanship.

    8 GB Extended RAM

    Enhanced system performance and seamless multitasking eliminate the need to manually quit apps running in the background. 8 GB Extended RAM for the 8 GB RAM edition increases the number of active background apps. Smoother switching between apps, instant running of background apps.

    Ultra-clear Imaging System

    Equipped with a 50 MP main camera, the device captures even more details. Whether it's bright or dark, trust our outstanding image processing to deliver ultra-sharp images full of intricacies. Clearer Images in Low Light, Your Exclusive Night Photography Partner.

    4K Video

    The Video mode for the rear camera supports recording 4K videos. It allows you to easily take sharper videos, making you the director of your own life.

    Super Night Mode

    Super Night Algorithms are optimized for the rear camera using noise cancellation and frame merging algorithms, empowering you to capture every wonderful nighttime moment with purer and more expressive images.

    IP64 Dust and Water Resistant

    IP64 Dust and Water Resistance for a More Reliable Experience. Capable of withstanding pure water being splashed, sprayed, or directly poured onto it. This phone is built to withstand the elements, keeping your adventures worry-free.
