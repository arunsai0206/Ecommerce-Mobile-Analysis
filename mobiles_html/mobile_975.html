    Product Description
    Leica 50 MP Professional Camera System

    The Leica 50 MP Professional Camera System, which consists of three Leica cameras built to produce stunning portraits, wide-angle ultra-wide pictures, and dramatic HDR, is the brains behind the Xiaomi 14 CIVI. With the Xiaomi 14 CIVI, you can capture daily events that turn into amazing movie scenes and professional-quality photos, all while paying homage to the legendary Leica lineage with every image. The camera arrangement is designed for individuals who aspire to record the world with unmatched creative flair and clarity, guaranteeing that each picture and video is exceptional in terms of detail and colour accuracy.

    Dual Selfie AI Camera

    The front-facing camera technology on the Xiaomi 14 CIVI is truly remarkable. It has two 32MP lenses: an ultra-wide lens that automatically shifts to capture more of your surroundings, ideal for group photos, and a beauty lens for gorgeous selfies. With its sophisticated AI face identification and astute upgrades, the Dual Selfie AI Camera makes sure you look your best in every photo. This camera adjusts to give you the best picture every time, whether you're taking a quick selfie or recording an excursion with pals.

    4K Video Recording

    With the Xiaomi 14 CIVI's 4K video recording capabilities, you can vlog and film videos at a whole new level. Take use of professional movie options that make every shot lively and captivating, such as composition tracking, auto-focus switching, and auto-zoom capabilities. With the cinema-level recording possibilities provided by the Master Cinema Mode and Pro LUTs, you may produce visually spectacular footage that can compete with that of experienced filmmakers. Every moment can be turned into a cinematic masterpiece with the Xiaomi 14 CIVI.

    Floating Quad Curve Design

    The Xiaomi 14 CIVI presents the only quad-curve display in the market, combining flat screen functionality with a tasteful curved design. The 1.5K 120 Hz AMOLED display, with its vibrant colours, crisp contrasts, and fluid movements, guarantees an unparalleled viewing experience. Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 protects this industry-leading colour gamut display, ensuring endurance without sacrificing visual quality. The display vividly and clearly brings every image to life, whether you're perusing through social media or viewing your favourite shows.

    Ultra Sleek Design

    The Xiaomi 14 CIVI has been created with elegance in mind. It is lightweight (177 g) and has a thin body measuring just 7.4 mm. This elegant, curving body is made from premium, eco-friendly materials.

    Third-generation Snapdragon 8s Mobile Platform

    The Pascal T1 chipset, which improves connection, is combined with the third-generation Snapdragon 8s mobile platform to power the Xiaomi 14 CIVI. With its twin flagship chipset arrangement, you can multitask and play intense games at the same time, all with lightning-fast speeds. With a maximum RAM of 16 GB and a maximum storage capacity of 512 GB, the Xiaomi 14 CIVI guarantees that you will have room and speed to tackle your daily tasks.

    Double Battery Lifespan

    With the powerful 4700 mAh battery of the Xiaomi 14 CIVI, which can run the device for up to 1.4 days on a single charge, you can wave goodbye to battery anxiety. You can swiftly recharge and resume your day without skipping a beat, thanks to the 67 W Turbo Charge technology. This modern battery technology guarantees 1600 charge cycles, so even after 4.3 years of intensive usage, your battery's health will remain at 80% or higher. You obtain a long-lasting gadget with the Xiaomi 14 CIVI that performs dependably every day.

    Powered by Xiaomi HyperOS

    The customised, potent, and sophisticated operating system known as Xiaomi HyperOS powers the Xiaomi 14 CIVI and completely transforms how you engage with the digital world. Xiaomi HyperOS guarantees a smooth, safe, and easy-to-use user experience with live designs, a customised user interface, a separate security chip, and improved interconnectivity. Whether you're using Xiaomi HyperOS to customise your smartphone, manage your smart home, or protect your data, it adjusts to your needs to make every interaction seamless and pleasurable.
