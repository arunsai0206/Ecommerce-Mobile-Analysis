    Product Description
    Enhanced Display

    Featuring a curved 3D AMOLED display of up to 16.94 cm, this Lava Blaze X smartphone provides an immersive viewing experience. With up to a 120 Hz refresh rate, you can enjoy smooth visuals and effortless scrolling, whether you're gaming, watching videos, or browsing social media. This display technology ensures vibrant colours and clarity for an enhanced visual experience.

    Functional Usage

    You can capture life’s moments in detail with the dual Sony rear camera setup of this smartphone, which includes up to 64 MP and 2 MP lenses. The front camera, with up to 16 MP, is ideal for selfies and video calls. This smartphone offers versatile camera modes such as dual view video, film, pro video, slow motion, time -lapse, UHD, GIF, beauty, HDR, night, portrait, AI, panorama, filters, and macro camera options, allowing you to explore your creativity and capture high-quality photos and videos.

    Fast Efficiency

    Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 5G Octa-core Processor, this smartphone ensures fast and seamless performance. It offers up to 128 GB ROM and expandable RAM, providing ample storage space for all your apps, files, and multimedia. Equipped with Android 14, it includes features like anonymous auto-call recording for added convenience and security.

    Practical Features

    This smartphone supports up to 33 W fast charging, dual SIM functionality, and a long-lasting 5,000 mAh battery to keep you connected throughout the day. With in-display fingerprint and face unlock, it offers secure and hassle-free access to your device, ensuring practical convenience in everyday use. Thus, this smartphone can be a smart choice for anyone who is looking for convenience and efficiency.
