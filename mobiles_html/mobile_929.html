    Product Description
    Impressive Display

    With a 17.32 cm screen and a resolution of 3168x1440, this smartphone offers an expansive viewing area. The clarity and sharpness of the display enhance readability, making it suitable for reading, browsing, and watching videos. The touchscreen feature ensures easy navigation through apps and settings. Enjoy various content types with greater detail and vibrant colours.

    Powerful Processor

    Equipped with an Octa-Core processor with a primary clock speed of 3.3GHz, the device handles multitasking smoothly. The processor allows for lightning-fast performance and seamless multitasking. Whether you're gaming, streaming videos, or running multiple apps at once, this smartphone can handle it all without a break. This reliable processing unit supports demanding use cases, making it apt for work and recreational purposes alike.

    Extended Battery Life

    This smartphone comes equipped with an impressive battery capacity of 5400 mAh, ensuring long-lasting usage for all your needs. Whether you're browsing the web, streaming videos, or playing games, this high-capacity battery allows for extended usage without constantly needing to recharge. With such a powerful battery, the OnePlus 12 is the perfect companion for those who are always on the go.

    High-resolution Rear Camera

    The OnePlus 12 smartphone boasts a powerful 64MP rear camera with dual camera lenses, taking photography to the next level. With this impressive feature, photography enthusiasts can capture stunning images with clarity and enhanced details. The dual camera system enables users to experiment with various photography modes and effects, ensuring they can unleash their creativity and capture perfect shots every time.

    Ample Storage

    Providing 256 GB of internal storage, the smartphone offers significant space for apps, files, and media. This ample storage capacity supports a variety of uses, ensuring that you can store large volumes of data without worrying about space limitations. The 12 GB RAM enhances the performance, enabling smooth functioning during intensive tasks.
