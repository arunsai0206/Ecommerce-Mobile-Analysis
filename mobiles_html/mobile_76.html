    Product Description
    Incredible Camera

    Replay life's best bits again and again with the Phone (3a)'s camera system that consistently delivers true-to-life results. Captures every light and shadow in stunning detail for elite-level shots, wherever you are. Boasting a large, light-absorbing 50 MP sensor with the largest full well capacity in its class, a high-res 50 MP telephoto sensor with up to 30X ultra zoom, a 32 MP front camera for superstar groufies, and a 120º ultra-wide field of view. All four sensors support Ultra HDR photo output.

    Hello, Snapdragon

    Experience unmatched power and efficiency with the latest Snapdragon chipset, crafted on cutting-edge TSMC 4nm technology. Equipped with the upgraded Qualcomm Kryo CPU architecture and Qualcomm Adreno 810 GPU, it delivers an 8% boost in power efficiency compared to the Phone (2a), offering up to 0.5 hours of extra battery life. The performance takes a leap forward with a 33% increase in CPU power and an 11% GPU upgrade over the Phone (2a), achieving an impressive AnTuTu score of 825,564. From seamless multitasking to ultra-smooth mobile gaming, this chipset ensures every experience is fast, responsive, and effortless.

    High-end Visuals

    Phone (3a) has a flexible FHD+ 17.19 cm (6.77) AMOLED display that can deliver stunningly accurate colour reproduction of 1.07 billion colours. Boasting a peak brightness of 3000 nits, which is our brightest screen, ensuring superb visibility even in direct sunlight. It also guarantees the display of Ultra HDR photos, utilizing the highest brightness to showcase highlights in images, such as blue skies, lights, and more

    Android Supremacy

    Nothing OS delivers a fast and smooth Android experience rooted in utility and functional customization, streamlining your smartphone use for a more intuitive experience. Customize your navigation by selecting a monochromatic theme to reduce distractions or removing app labels for a cleaner look. Our expanding widget library breathes new life into your lock and home screens, while we continuously optimize software for Phone (3a) and Phone (3a) Pro to ensure the fastest, most fluid experience.

    The Ultimate Hub

    Say goodbye to the hassle of juggling multiple apps for your important notes, images, and inspirations. With the new Essential Key, you can effortlessly grab photos, record your screen, and capture your voice—all in one place. Everything you capture is stored in your Essential Space, an AI-powered sanctuary that organizes your notes, ideas, and inspirations. The system recognizes content, transcribes text and audio, and describes images, automatically sorting and cataloging everything for easy access. Plus, you can create custom groups for your projects and ideas, making it simpler than ever to keep your digital life organized and ready for action. Ask, listen, and learn from ChatGPT on the go. Get the answers you need faster and at your fingertips. Search text, voice, or image right from your home screen.

    Charge Fast

    At 5000 mAh, Phone (3a) contains our biggest battery to date, delivering up to two days of use on a full charge. Not only is it larger, it also performs better; maintaining over 90% of its maximum capacity after 1,200 charging cycles, corresponding to over 3 years of daily charging. Contributing to its longevity is the reduced temperature of the battery while charging and discharging. For rapid power-ups, Phone (3a) supports 50W Fast Charging, delivering a day of power in under 20 minutes – nearly 10% faster than Phone (2a). An optimal charging solution that avoids the risk of depleting battery life, often related to higher watt count and charging speeds.
