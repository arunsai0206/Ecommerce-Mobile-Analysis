    Product Description
    Custom Mediatek Dimensity 7200 Pro Processor

    Phone (2a) is fuelled by the custom Dimesity 7200 Pro chipset. Co-engineered between Nothing and MediaTek to deliver the best performance with optimal power consumption. Do more whilst using less thanks to game-changing efficiency from the 8-core 4nm TSMC 2nd Generation process, with clocking speeds of up to 2.8 GHz for multi-tasking to the max. Plus get up to 20 GB RAM with 8 GB RAM Booster.

    Advanced Rear Camera

    Capture life as it's happening. Phone (2a) packs a 50 MP main sensor with nearly double the pixel well depth compared to Phone (1) This means that even in the most compromising conditions, contrast details can be captured with no blooming or smearing. Sits alongside a 50 MP ultra-wide sensor with a 114° field-of-view that brings out the best in every scene and group get-together.

    32 MP Selfie Camera

    You are in your best light. Land the perfect selfies anytime, anywhere with an exceptional 32 MP front camera that uses the same sensor as the Phone (2). Or self-record in immersive 1080p at 60 FPS.

    TrueLens Engine

    Beyond hardware, the TrueLens Engine features a range of advanced algorithms that ensure every shot, whatever the hour, is as close to real life as possible. Including Ultra XDR, which uses multi-frame technology to capture and display images with more accurate contrasts between highlights and shadows. Motion Capture, which makes action shots a walk in the park. And Night Mode for amplifying details in low-light environments.

    Vapour Chamber

    Balancing high performance with effective heat control, Phone (2a) incorporates an advanced liquid cooling system. The extra-large 3,200 mm² vapour chamber allows for faster heat dissipation by transferring it across the back of the device.

    17.01 cm (6.7) Flexible AMOLED Display

    Big, bright, brilliant. With structural innovations, this phone has narrowed down the symmetrical bezels to just 2.1 mm, creating a screen-to-body ratio of 91.65%. With a flexible, power-efficient AMOLED display, Phone (2a) can reproduce over 1 billion vivid colours with the deepest of contrasts. Packed with 700 nits of brightness, reaching 1300 nits at peak brightness. Delivers a 120 Hz refresh rate that dynamically adapts to your content for sublimely smooth interactions.

    Nothing OS 2.5, Powered by Android 14

    Experience the best of Android through a unique aesthetic that feels unmistakably Nothing. OS 2.5 delivers an experience that's faster and smoother than ever, rooted in utility and functional customisation. Do more directly from your home and lock screen by diving into the Nothing widget library. Little windows into your apps and most-used functions, now with scrollable views for more levels of information.

    The Iconic Glyph Interface

    Discover the Glyph Interface, a new, screen-free way to interact. Put your phone down to receive key information, in a flash. Now with a new trio-light configuration and 15 innovative functions. Beyond notifications and incoming calls, the Glyph Interface also serves as a volume indicator, a timer, and a countdown that operates in collaboration with third-party apps. So you can keep an eye on the progress of your delivery service without having to be on your screen.

    Sustainability

    A product of products. Nothing has developed a brand-new recycling process in its manufacturing plant, which allows for plastic waste from the Ear (2) production line to be repurposed into Phone ( 2a ). Nothing has also maintained their commitment to plastic-free packaging, using recycled fibre instead. Phone (2a) itself also contains a number of recycled materials: 100% recycled aluminium is used in the mid-frame. 100% recycled tin on 6 circuit boards. 100% recycled copper foil on the main circuit board. Recycled steel on 22 steel stamping parts. Over 50% of the plastic parts are sustainably sourced.
