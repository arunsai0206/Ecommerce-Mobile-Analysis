    Product Description
    MediaTek Dimensity 8400 Ultra

    Powered by the globally debuting MediaTek Dimensity 8400 Ultra chipset, the POCO X7 Pro leads the segment with an Antutu V10 score of 1.7M+. Its flagship-grade A725 CPU architecture, clocked up to 3.25 GHz, delivers a 20% boost in CPU and 25% in GPU performance compared to the previous generation, ensuring unparalleled speed and efficiency.

    Memory Technology with LPDDR5X + UFS 4.0

    Featuring LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.0 storage, the POCO X7 Pro ensures lightning-fast data access and seamless multitasking. With Wildboost 3.0, enjoy high refresh rates, stable brightness, and optimized image quality for a smooth and responsive experience.

    Xiaomi HyperOS 2.0

    The POCO X7 Pro debuts Xiaomi HyperOS 2.0, based on Android 15, combining flagship software and hardware for an optimized user experience. The device offers 3 years of Android updates and 4 years of security patches for long-term reliability.

    Massive Battery with Smart Charging

    The 6550mAh Silicon Carbon battery, coupled with 90W Turbo Charging, powers the POCO X7 Pro to 100% in just ~47 minutes. AI-driven power-saving features adapt to your usage, while smart charging technology ensures reliable performance even at extreme temperatures as low as -20°C.

    Stunning 1.5K pOLED Display

    The 1.5K pOLED display delivers a perfect blend of FHD+ and 2K resolution with vibrant colors and sharp details. Offering a peak brightness of 3200nits, Dolby Vision certification, and adaptive refresh rates (30Hz/60Hz/90 Hz /120Hz), the display provides an immersive viewing experience while conserving power.

    Durable Design with Corning Gorilla Glass 7i

    Built to last, the POCO X7 Pro features Corning Gorilla Glass 7i for enhanced drop and scratch resistance. It passed TUV SUD and IP66 + IP68 + IP69(supports) test, it is designed to endure tough conditions.

    50MP Sony LYT-600 Camera System

    Capture stunning details with the 50MP Sony LYT-600 primary camera, equipped with OIS and EIS for sharp and bright photos. Its advanced AI Portrait Mode, Ultra Clear Zoom, and MFNR deliver professional-grade images. The 20MP AI-enhanced selfie camera ensures perfect shots with features like group photo optimization and beautification options.

    Video Mode

    OIS & EIS are both supported when recording 4K/60fps for a perfect combination of high picture quality and high frame rates. The Dual Video Mode allows simultaneous recording from the front and rear cameras, making it a favorite among content creators. Whether vlogging or creating dynamic videos, this feature enhances creativity and versatility.

    AI Interpreter

    Effortlessly break language barriers with the AI Interpreter. This cutting-edge feature translates live calls and meetings into your desired language in real time. Its advanced speaker recognition can distinguish between multiple participants, ensuring seamless and accurate communication for global collaborations.

    AI Notes

    Revolutionize the way you take notes with AI Notes. Equipped with AI-powered tools like Summarize, Proofread, Translate, and Layout, this feature elevates your productivity. From condensing lengthy content to polishing grammar and organizing your notes for clarity, AI Notes makes staying organized and efficient easier than ever.

    AI on Gallery

    Transform your photos effortlessly with AI Magic Eraser Pro and Image Expansion. Remove unwanted elements seamlessly while extending image boundaries with realistic, intelligent additions. Craft perfect visuals and unlock new creative possibilities in just a few taps.

    AI Recorder

    Transform the way you record and transcribe with AI Recording. Ideal for content creators and professionals, this feature converts your audio recordings into accurate text, saving time and effort. Whether creating content or documenting important conversations, AI Recording ensures a seamless workflow. Also with AI Subtitles, the tool listens to your audio in real-time and provides translations in your preferred language. It also summarizes discussions automatically, helping you capture key points from meetings or classes without missing a beat.
