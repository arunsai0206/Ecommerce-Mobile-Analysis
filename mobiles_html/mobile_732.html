    • Description
    The Lava Yuva Star is an affordable smartphone with key features designed for budget-conscious users. It sports a 6.75-inch HD+ display, one of the largest in its price range, offering an immersive viewing experience. The device is powered by 4 GB RAM and 64 GB internal storage, providing sufficient performance for everyday tasks and expandable storage for more space. The 13 MP AI Dual rear camera delivers good photo quality with AI optimization, while the 5000 mAh battery ensures long-lasting usage, perfect for all-day performance. The phone also includes security features like Secure Face Unlock and a fingerprint reader, enabling quick and secure access. Included in the box are essential accessories such as a charger and a phone cover, adding extra value. Overall, the Lava O3 is a well-rounded budget smartphone offering a large display, decent camera, and reliable battery life at an affordable price.
