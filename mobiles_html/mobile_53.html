    Product Description
    Ultra Slim and Light yet Durable with MIL-810H Miliary Graded Certified

    Certified to MIL-STD-810H by SGS, this device meets the U.S. Department of Defense's rigorous standards for durability. It can withstand shocks, vibrations, pressure, dust, extreme temperatures, and salt fog.

    Sleek and Balanced Design

    The Motorola Edge 50 meets military standards for durability, all while maintaining a sleek, perfectly balanced design. Crafted with a sense of harmony—featuring vegan leather and vegan suede finishes, and comfortably curved edges.

    50 MP Ultra Pixel Camera with Sony LYTIA 700C Sensor

    The 50MP Ultra Pixel camera with Sony LYTIA 700C sensor captures vivid, detailed images with advanced night photography, optical image stabilization, and diverse lens options, including a telephoto, 13 MP ultrawide, and 32 MP selfie camera.

    30x Hybrid Zoom Telephoto Camera

    Capture distant subjects in detail with the dedicated telephoto camera, offering 3x optical zoom and 30x hybrid zoom with Optical Image Stabilization for clear shots. Moto AI enhances details for crisp results even at maximum zoom.

    Google Photos Integration

    Google Photos is a hub for your photos and videos, offering automatic backup and organization. It features powerful editing and AI tools like Magic Editor, Magic Eraser, and Photo Unblur to effortlessly enhance your photos.

    Immersive Display and Superior Audio

    Immerse yourself in the nearly borderless 16.94 cm (6.67) pOLED display with Super HD (1220p) resolution. Enjoy HDR10+ content with vivid colors and infinite contrast. Experience rich, multidimensional sound with Dolby Atmos and large stereo speakers for superior audio quality.

    Snapdragon 7 Gen 1 Accelerated Edition Performance

    The Snapdragon 7 Gen 1 Accelerated Edition offers top-tier performance with 4nm technology, AI-powered features, pro-level photography, and efficient power usage. With storage options of 256GB or 512GB, and up to 12GB RAM, you’ll have ample space and smooth multitasking. RAM Boost temporarily converts storage into virtual RAM for faster app switching.

    TurboPower and Wireless Charging

    The Moto Edge 50 features 68W TurboPower for rapid charging, delivering a full day’s power in just 15 minutes. Its 5000 mAh battery offers over 30 hours of use. It also supports up to 15 W TurboPower wireless charging.
