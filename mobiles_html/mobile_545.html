    Product Description
    Attractive Display Quality

    Experience vibrant visuals with the attractive display quality of the OnePlus 12R. The generous display size of 17.22cm allows for an immersive viewing experience. With a resolution of 2780*1264 pixels, every image, video, and game comes to life with sharp detail and clarity. Enjoy a truly sensational visual experience on the OnePlus 12R's stunning screen.

    Long Battery Life

    Experience long-lasting battery life with the impressive 5500mAh battery of the OnePlus 12R. This feature allows you to go all day without worrying about having to constantly recharge your device. Whether you're a heavy user or just need a reliable phone for everyday tasks, the OnePlus 12R's extended battery life ensures that you stay connected and productive wherever you go.

    Extensive Storage

    Experience the maximum speed and smooth operation with the unbeatable 16GB internal RAM, coupled with an impressive 256GB internal storage on the OnePlus 12R. Store all your important files, photos, videos and apps without worrying about running out of space. Enjoy the benefits of extensive storage capabilities and never compromise on performance with this cutting-edge smartphone.

    Advanced Processor

    The OnePlus 12R boast an advanced processor with an Octa-Core design, allowing for smooth performance and efficient multitasking capabilities. With a primary clock speed of 3.2 GHz, this device offers lightning-fast processing speeds for a seamless user experience. Whether you’re gaming, streaming, or completing work tasks, the OnePlus 12R delivers top-notch performance.

    VC Cooling System

    The Dual Cryo-velocity VC cooling system of the OnePlus 12R ensures optimal performance even during intense gaming sessions. By combining two high-powered fans with state-of-the-art cooling technology, this system effectively dissipates heat to prevent throttling and maintain peak performance. Whether you're streaming videos or playing graphics-heavy games, the Dual Cryo-velocity VC cooling system keeps your OnePlus 10R running smoothly and cool.
