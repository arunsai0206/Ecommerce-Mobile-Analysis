    Product Description
    50 MP Ultra Pixel OIS Camera with Sony - LYTIA 700C Senor

    Motorola edge 50 fusion is designed to capture its best shots at night with advanced features. The 50 MP main camera uses a powerful Sony LYTIA LYT-700C sensor to capture moments ultrafast with low noise even in dark environments. Who thought Night photography could get faster, accurate and focused. An advanced all-pixel focus technology on the Motorola Edge 50 Fusion elevates night photography by making it 32x more focused. While the Ultra Pixel sensor captures more light with larger 2.0µm pixels, the OIS automatically smoothens involuntary hand motion to give you a shake free video experience.

    4K Video Recording in 13MP Ultrawide+Macro Cam and 32 MP Front Cam

    Its advanced sensor gives two amazing perspectives. The 120 degree ultra-wide-angle lens captures what your eyes see, fitting more of the scene in your frame than a standard lens. So go ultra-wide or zoom in. The Macro Vision lens brings you 4x closer to your subject, so you see the tiniest of details. The new Quad Pixel technology combines every four pixels into one for 4x better low-light sensitivity giving you brilliant results. Moreover, you can also capture videos in 4K resolution from all cameras front and back and never miss a detail.

    IP68 Underwater Protection with Smart Water Touch

    Go worry-free on your adventures with the Motorola edge IP68 underwater protection, engineered to withstand dust, dirt, and sand, along with submersion in 1.5 meters of freshwater for up to 30 minutes. Now, getting wet is just one less thing to worry about.  Worry free display usage with Smart Water Touch technology.

    Beautifully Crafted Endless Edge Design in Vegan Leather Finish

    Motorola edge 50 fusion comes with an impressive design language. The symmetrical curved inlays align perfectly with your hand’s natural contours. At the same time, the endless edge design gives the ultrathin, lightweight body a sleek, refined look. Available in Hot Pink vegan suede or a Marshmallow Blue vegan leather finish that gives it a soft, tactile feel with an added touch of luxury. And another version highlights the sophisticated PMMA finish in Forest Blue.

    144 Hz 3D Curved Display with Gorilla Glass 5 Protection

    This 6.67 inch Curved display makes your content viewing even more immerse as it gives you continuous view from edge to edge. Experience over a billion shades of true-to-life colour with improved brightness and contrast. Enjoy it all with a remarkably smooth 144 Hz refresh rate. See better, even when you're outside with 1600 nits peak brightness, and enjoy life-like content HDR10+ support. The Corning Gorilla Glass 5 Scratch-resistant screen protects your display from damage. While the symmetrical curved inlay makes it comfortable to hold, aligning perfectly with your hand’s natural contours. Even the camera housing blends seamlessly with symmetrically curved sides.

    Snapdragon 7s Gen 2 and upto 12 GB RAM

    Accelerate performance with the Snapdragon 7s Gen 2 processor, powering advanced photography features and intensive gaming—all with unbelievable battery efficiency. This phone comes with upto 12 GB of memory with RAM Boost upto additional 12 GB. Store upto 256 GB of photos, videos, and games without breaking a sweat and turn your unused storage into virtual RAM upto 12 GB whenever you need some extra speed using RAM Boost. There is also 8 GB RAM + 128 GB storage variant for more affordable price.

    68 W TurboPower Charging with 5000 mAh Battery

    Get 5000 mAh battery in this ultra thin design. Power your day in just 15 minutes with 68 W TurboPower fast charging support. Which means 30 hours of battery life for all the crazy things you want to explore. Binge-watch that new series, stay in touch with friends, and capture the world around you—day and night.

    Hello UI Based Android 14 with 3 Years OS Update

    Customise your smartphone with your personal touch with the Hello UI based on Latest Android 14 out of the box. Change the colour schemes, themes, and languages of your apps and get complete control over which apps can access your data. You can also make your Moto more intuitive, more personal and more secure with exclusive Moto features. Moto Secure protects your phone and keeps all the threats at bay. Additionally, you will receive guaranteed 3 years OS updates till Android 17 and 4 years of security updates.
