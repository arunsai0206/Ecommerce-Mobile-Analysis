    Product Description
    AI Suite - Advance AI Features

    The AI Suite's advanced features harness AI to enhance digital content creation: AIGC for Generative AI Avatars, AI Eraser to seamlessly remove image elements, AI Cut Out for precise subject extraction, AI Wallpaper for generating custom device backgrounds via text and images, AI Artboard for assisted artistic design, and Ask AI for interactive, creative guidance. These tools collectively offer a powerful, user-friendly experience for content creators of all skill levels.

    108 MP AI Camera

    The Segment 1st 5G smartphone, with its 108 MP AI main camera, captures ultra-clear, high-resolution images that boast vivid details and rich textures, elevating photography to professional levels. The triple LED flash on the back ensures superior illumination in low-light conditions, while the integrated light sensor adjusts the exposure for consistently great shots. The front features an 8 MP selfie camera with a Dual LED flash, enhancing the quality of front-facing photos even in dimly lit environments. This combination of advanced imaging technology makes the Segment 1st 5G a standout choice for photo enthusiasts.

    Bigger Memory and Storage

    This 5G smartphone boasts a high-performance variant equipped with 16 GB of RAM and 256 GB of storage. It features innovative memory fusion technology that effectively doubles the RAM capacity for enhanced multitasking. The device utilizes speedy LPDDR4X RAM and UFS2.2 ROM types, ensuring swift and efficient data processing and storage.

    Powerful Performance

    The Pova 6 Neo 5G is outfitted with the MediaTek D6300 Octa-core processor, which incorporates a 6 nm chipset clocked at 2.4 GHz. This setup is designed to deliver a quick and smooth user experience, aiming for lag-free performance over a period of up to 5 years, catering to users who prioritize sustained efficiency in their mobile devices.

    120 Hz Smooth Display

    The Pova 6 Neo 5G features a 16.94 cm (6.67) display with a 120 Hz refresh rate, delivering smooth visuals and a responsive user experience, perfect for gaming and media. This high refresh rate with a 16.94 cm (6.67) large display ensures seamless motion and crisp graphics, providing users with a premium viewing experience that is unparalleled in its class. Scroll lots of reels, videos, and tons of photos in one go.

    Durability Battery and Endurance

    The Pova 6 Neo 5G is designed for endurance, featuring a robust 5000 mAh battery complemented by 18 W fast charging, providing long-lasting battery life and efficient recharging to support your daily activities. Additionally, it boasts an IP54 rating, indicating a level of protection against dust ingress and splashing water, enhancing the device's durability for everyday use.

    Efficiency-driven Features

    The Pova 6 Neo 5G is also equipped with an infrared sensor, enabling remote control functionalities for compatible devices, and features an all-directional NFC for versatile contactless connectivity and payments. Additionally, it is enhanced with Dolby Atmos technology, delivering an immersive audio experience with rich, dynamic sound quality.
