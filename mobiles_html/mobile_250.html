    Product Description
    Guaranteed Durablity with MIL-810H Miliary Graded Certified

    Certified to MIL-STD-810H by SGS, this device meets the U.S. Department of Defense's tough durability standards. It's built to endure shocks, vibrations, pressure, dust, extreme temperatures, and salt fog

    Minimal Design with PANTONE Colours

    Designed in conjunction with Pantone Color Institute, the world’s authority on color, Motorola Edge 50 Neo is available in four specially curated options: PANTONE Poinciana, PANTONE Grisaille, PANTONE Nautical Blue, and PANTONE Latte.

    50 MP Quad Pixel Camera with Sony LYTIA 700C Sensor

    The 50MP Ultra Pixel camera, featuring the Sony LYTIA 700C sensor, uses Quad Pixel Technology to deliver vivid, detailed images. It excels in night photography with optical image stabilization and offers versatile lens options, including a telephoto, a 13 MP ultrawide, and a 32 MP selfie camera.

    10 MP Telephoto Camera with 30X AI Super Zoom

    Capture distant subjects with precision using the dedicated telephoto camera, featuring 3x optical zoom and 30x hybrid zoom. With Optical Image Stabilization and Moto AI, you’ll get sharp, detailed shots even at maximum zoom.

    A New Class of Display with 3000 nits Brightness

    The Motorola Edge 50 Neo’s 16.25 cm (6.4) pOLED LTPO Super HD display offers 1.5K resolution, HDR10+ brightness up to 3000 nits, deeper blacks, and cinematic colors with DCI-P3 and 10-bit. LTPO tech dynamically adjusts refresh rates, optimizing battery life.

    Google Photos Integration

    Google Photos is your go-to place for all your photos and videos, providing seamless backup and smart organization. With advanced AI tools like Magic Editor, Magic Eraser, and Photo Unblur, enhancing your photos has never been easier.

    TurboPower and Wireless Charging

    Never stop creating with the Moto Edge 50 Neo. Charge from 0% to 50% in just 15 minutes with 68 W TurboPower, and enjoy worry-free battery life with its 4310 mAh battery. Get up to X hours of playback, recording, or streaming in 12 minutes, plus 15W wireless charging for added convenience.

    MediaTek Dimensity 7300

    Equipped with the advanced MediaTek Dimensity 7300 and 12 GB RAM, this device ensures top performance for years. Enjoy five years of software support, Android upgrades, and AI innovations. With 512 GB storage (or 256GB), you have ample space for all your creations.

    Freedom with Hello UI Software

    Hello UI sets itself apart with unique features like Customization and Moto Unplugged. These elements resonate strongly with the audience, ensuring the software experience is communicated effectively, even without highlighting every detail.
