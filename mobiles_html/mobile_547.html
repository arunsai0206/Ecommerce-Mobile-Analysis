    Product Description
    A Canvas of Clarity

    The Galaxy A05 introduces you to a visual extravaganza with its expansive 17.13 cm (6.7) HD+ display. The screen, stretching from edge to edge, offers an immersive experience that brings your favourite games, movies, and content to life in stunning clarity and vibrant colours.

    Photography: A Trio of Perspectives

    At the heart of the Galaxy A05's photography prowess are its versatile triple cameras. The 50 MP Wide-angle Camera captures the world in breathtaking detail, the 2 MP Depth Camera adds layers to your portraits, and the 8 MP Front Camera ensures your selfies are always on point. Together, they create a photographic ensemble that lets you capture every angle and every emotion.

    Unleashing Power and Efficiency

    Under the hood, the Galaxy A05 is a powerhouse. Equipped with an octa-core processor, and available in 4 GB RAM variants with 64 GB of internal storage, it’s engineered for smooth, efficient performance. Whether gaming, browsing, or multitasking, the Galaxy A05 handles it all with effortless grace.

    Smart Memory Expansion

    Experience a fluid and responsive user experience with Galaxy A05's RAM Plus feature. It intelligently reads your usage pattern to provide extra virtual RAM, ensuring your apps and tasks run smoothly, even when juggling multiple at once.

    Endurance Redefined

    The 5,000 mAh battery of the Galaxy A05 is a testament to endurance. Designed for those who are always on the move, it ensures your day is filled with uninterrupted entertainment. And when you do need a boost, the 25 W Super Fast Charging swiftly brings your device back to full power.

    Effortless and Reliable

    Unlocking your Galaxy A05 is as simple as a glance. The advanced Face Unlock feature prioritises your privacy and makes accessing your phone quick and secure, freeing you from the hassle of passwords and patterns.

    Immersive and Authentic Audio Experience

    The 3.5 mm earphone jack on the Galaxy A05 stands for simplicity and quality. Coupled with Dolby Atmos technology, it promises an audio experience that is rich, immersive, and truly enveloping, placing you at the center of your music and movies.

    Software Updates

    With the promise of 2 OS upgrades and 4 years of security updates, your experience with the Galaxy A05 will only get better over time. These updates ensure your device stays at the forefront of technological advancements, keeping you secure and updated.

    Support and Care

    Owning a Samsung Galaxy A05 comes with the assurance of quality and support. The Samsung Members app provides interactive diagnostics and optimisation to keep your device performing at its peak, while the Samsung helpline is always ready to assist with any queries or issues.

    Personalised and Intuitive User Interface

    The One UI Core on the Galaxy A05 offers a personalised and intuitive user interface. It's designed to be fully customisable, allowing you to tailor your phone to your own style and preferences, making every interaction with your device uniquely yours.
