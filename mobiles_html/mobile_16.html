    Large Battery

    Go All Day , Every Day with no limits with vivo T4x 5G. The 6500 mAh battery and 44W FlashCharge deliver all-day energy, quick recharges, and smart efficiency for nonstop use.

    Using advanced AI technology, AI Photo Enhance automatically optimizes and enhances photos. It improves facial clarity, restores details like eyes and mouth, and enhances color performance, transforming ordinary photos and unclear old images into high-quality ones to bring beautiful moments back to life.

    Quad-curved edges on the back create a sleek, ergonomic design that fits perfectly in your hand and feels great to grip. With a thickness of a mere 0.809 cm, the phone is comfortable to hold in your hand, even for long hours.

    Ultra Game Mode
