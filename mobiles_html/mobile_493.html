    Brilliant Connectivity

    With the Samsung Galaxy A23 5G, you can enjoy the power of 5G fast speeds that transforms how you consume and share information, enabling incredibly fluid gaming and streaming as well as lightning-quick uploading and downloading.

    Unmatched Performance

    With up to 6 GB of RAM, the Galaxy A23 5G combines the strength of the Snapdragon mobile platform with quick and effective performance for the work at hand.

    Dazzling Display

    You get extra space to view and do things on the 16.76 cm (6.6) Infinity-V Display of the Galaxy A23 5G.

    Elegant Design

    The camera is connected to the matte finish on the back of the Galaxy A23 5G via the Ambient Edge design, giving it a distinctive appearance.

    Mesmerising Images

    With the 50 MP OIS Camera, you can take clear, sharp pictures. Furthermore, with the Ultra Wide Camera, you can widen your field of view. Additionally, with the Depth Camera, you can adjust the focus, and with the Macro Camera, you can get closer to details.

    No Shake Cam

    Your Galaxy A23 5G films movies fluidly and catches fine details even in low-light conditions thanks to a No Shake Cam equipped into this phone.

    Ultra Wide Camera

    The 5 MP Ultra Wide Camera perceives the world with a 123-degree field of view, which is similar to human eyesight and gives everything you capture tremendous context.

    2 MP Depth Camera

    You can adjust the depth of focus in your images with the 2 MP depth camera included in this phone.

    Stupendous Front Camera

    You can take breathtaking selfies that emphasise you more than the background thanks to the bokeh effect that has been incorporated into the 8 MP front camera.

    Powerful Battery

    This phone's 5,000 mAh battery enables you to continue working for hours on end, which greatly increases your productivity.

    Game Booster

    Thanks to this phone's Game Booster technology, which detects your gaming habits, optimising the temperature, memory, and battery life of your smartphone, allowing you to enjoy smart gaming sessions.

    Dolby Atmos

    You can hear a sound with Dolby Atmos that is rich, powerful, and seems to engulf you in the action. You can enjoy your favourite songs in crystal-clear clarity in this way.
