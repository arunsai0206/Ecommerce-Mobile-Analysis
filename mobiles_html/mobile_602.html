    Dimensity 8350 Ultra 5G Chipset

    Powered by a cutting-edge chipset MediaTek Dimensity 8350 Ultra 5G, this phone delivers lightning-fast speed and seamless performance. Whether you're gaming, multitasking, or streaming, the ultra-efficient processor ensures smooth navigation, quick app launches, and reduced lag. Experience faster load times, enhanced AI capabilities, and power efficiency that keeps you going longer. With this powerhouse chipset, your phone isn't just fast—it’s unstoppable.

    4K 60 FPS Video Camera

    With a 4K 60 FPS video camera, this phone lets you record life in breathtaking clarity and smooth motion. Every detail pops with ultra-sharp resolution, rich colors, and cinematic fluidity. Whether you're shooting fast-paced action or stunning landscapes, the advanced stabilization and high frame rate ensure crystal-clear visuals with no blur. Create, capture, and relive every moment in true-to-life quality.

    1.5K Quad Curve Display 0.738 cm Slimmness

    Experience unmatched clarity with the 1.5K Quad-Curve Display, designed for vivid colors, sharp details, and an ultra-smooth viewing experience. The 0.738 cm slim design not only enhances the phone’s sleek aesthetics but also ensures a comfortable grip. With immersive visuals and a premium feel, this phone blends cutting-edge display technology with a refined, ultra-thin profile—delivering both beauty and performance in one.

    6000 mAh Battery + 80 W Charge

    With a massive 6000 mAh battery, this phone is built to keep up with you all day and beyond—whether you're gaming, streaming, or working on the go. And when it’s time to recharge, the 80 W charging gets you back to full power in no time. Say goodbye to battery anxiety and enjoy unstoppable performance with a device that’s always ready when you are.

    IP69 Water Resistance

    Engineered for ultimate durability, this phone comes with IP69 water resistance, making it tough enough to handle extreme conditions. Whether it's heavy rain, accidental spills, or even full submersion, your device stays protected. Designed to endure dust, water, and rugged environments, this is the phone that keeps up with your adventures—no matter where life takes you.

    AeroSpace Cooling System

    Equipped with an advanced Aerospace Cooling System, this phone ensures peak performance without overheating. Whether you're gaming, streaming, or multitasking, the cutting-edge cooling technology efficiently dissipates heat, keeping your device smooth and responsive. Experience uninterrupted power, faster processing, and a cooler grip—no matter how intense the action gets.

    AI Bypass Charging

    With AI Bypass Charging, this phone ensures efficient power management while keeping heat levels in check. By intelligently directing power straight to the system instead of the battery during heavy usage, it prevents overheating and extends the battery's lifespan. Whether you're gaming or streaming while plugged in, enjoy a cooler, safer, and more efficient charging experience.
