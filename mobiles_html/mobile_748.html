    Studio-quality Aura Light Portrait

    Illuminate your moments with the Studio-quality Aura Light Portrait feature. The Aura Light not only captures your essence but delicately preserves the texture of your skin, ensuring you radiate in every shot. Imagine capturing candid moments at a sunset beach party – the Aura Light Portrait effortlessly enhances your glow, making memories as vibrant as they felt.

No more squeezing in the middle; capture the essence of your group effortlessly. Picture this: a family reunion where everyone, from the elders to the kids, looks picture-perfect without the chaos of strategic positioning.

The sensor captures information akin to the human eye, enhancing colour accuracy in every shot. Imagine capturing a vibrant market scene – the VCS True Colour Main Camera ensures your photos mirror the vivid reality.

    The front camera isn't just for solo selfies; it's a powerhouse for group shots. With an industry-leading 50 MP resolution, upgraded wide-angle lens, and standard autofocus, the front camera captures brilliant group selfies. Imagine a spontaneous outing with friends – the 50 MP Eye AF Group Selfie turns every moment into a visual masterpiece, making memories as lively as the moment.

    The V30 Series achieves a delicate balance – exceptional battery capacity meets the light and slim design with a 5000 mAh battery. Imagine holding a device that not only powers your day but complements your style, effortlessly blending elegance with functionality.
