    Product Description
    Internal Storage

    With 128 GB of internal storage, the device gives you multiple benefits. You have ample space for apps, including the ones for photo-editing, gaming, online shopping, and work meetings. The storage space prevents you from deleting your downloaded apps and favourite photos to accommodate space for important files. Also, the internal storage prevents slowing of the device as you download apps or files.

    Durable Build

    This device has several features that offer durability and long-lasting performance. The back crystal glass design makes the device sturdy and helps to absorb impact of accidental falls. IP53 protection minimises the damage on water exposure and splashes. The gorilla glass makes the device scratch-resistant and helps to maintain aesthetic appeal and functional integrity. The wet touch display enables you to use the touchscreen even with wet or sweaty hands.

    Camera with Ring Flash

    The device has 108 MP camera which gives you several benefits. It gives you clearer images and thus, lets you capture minute details in every photo. The camera also has ring flash which gives better illumination and prevents chances of getting blurred images in low light. The camera, coupled with the ring flash, ensures that you can capture nature-looking photos in different lighting conditions. As a result, you can capture the special moments of your life with a vivid clarity.

    Snapdragon 4 Gen2 Accelerated Processor

    Get your personal and professional work done swiftly. With the Snapdragon 4 Gen2 accelerated edition octa-core processor, the phone offers several advantages. It enables you to download several apps without making the device slow and lets you enjoy faster internet speeds. This ensures you stay connected with your loved ones and can enjoy seamless gaming and other forms of entertainment. Overall, it gives you a better user experience and lets you enjoy the convenience of a fast-paced life.

    FHD+ Display

    The FHD+ display offers several benefits and helps you meet your needs. It offers better screen resolution and enables you to view texts, images, and videos with clearer details. This implies that you will have an enhanced experience of watching movies, playing games, or browsing online on the device. With a balance of power efficiency and image quality, it gives you brighter details and lower power consumption.

    5030 mAh Battery

    With 5030 mAh battery, the phone offers you multiple advantages. The battery lasts long and lets you browse through social media and stream videos for longer. The 33 W fast charging reduces waiting time and lets you stay in touch with work appointments and your loved ones. The device thus becomes not only suited for fast-paced life but also for travelling when charging facilities may not be frequently found.

    Side Fingerprint Sensor

    The positioning of the sensor makes it easily accessible without making it tricky to find during times of hurry. It ensures a more comfortable and also faster way of unlocking the device. Apart from this, the objective of the sensor lies in giving you enhanced security and safeguarding your data and sensitive information. The side fingerprint sensor prevents your data from getting into the hands of non-authorised users.
