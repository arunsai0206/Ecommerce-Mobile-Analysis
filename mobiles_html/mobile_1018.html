    Dimensity 7300 Energy Chipset

    Unleash unparalleled power with the realme P1 Speed 5G, featuring the cutting-edge Dimensity 7300 Energy. Boasting a revolutionary 4-core architecture clocked at 2.5GHz and built on an advanced 4nm process, it achieves an impressive Antutu score of 750,000. Enjoy a 25% boost in multi-core performance and a 30% reduction in power consumption, ensuring faster processing and extended battery life. Gamers will love the 20% increase in GPU performance, along with a 46% drop in power usage, delivering smooth frame rates and a captivating gaming experience. For those seeking the ultimate in mid-range performance, the realme P1 Speed 5G sets a new benchmark.

    45 W Ultra Charge with a 5000 mAh Battery

    The realme P1 Speed 5G redefines charging performance and battery endurance with its 45 W Ultra Charging and advanced battery technology. Equipped with a powerful 5000 mAh battery, it ensures you’re always in the game. Just 5 minutes of charging provides an hour of gameplay, keeping you in the action without interruptions. The unique combination of rapid charging and high-capacity battery offers exceptional performance for extended and immersive team battles. Stay powered up and ready for victory, no matter how intense the competition gets.

    6050 mm² Stainless Steel Vapor Cooling System

    The realme P1 Speed 5G boasts the segment's largest 6050mm² Stainless Steel Vapor Cooling System, engineered for exceptional thermal management. Its advanced closed VC structure and two-way cyclic convective technology maximize the cooling area, efficiently covering key heat sources. This comprehensive cooling solution delivers a significant upgrade in heat dissipation compared to competitors, ensuring stable performance and seamless operation during intense gaming or multitasking. With superior cooling capability, the realme P1 Speed 5G remains cool and responsive, no matter how demanding the conditions.

    24 GB DRAM + 256 GB ROM

    The realme P1 Speed 5G sets a new benchmark with the largest memory in its segment, featuring a massive 26GB DRAM (12GB LPDDR4X + 14GB Virtual RAM) paired with 256GB UFS 3.1 storage, delivering unparalleled performance and capacity. It supports up to 32 apps running simultaneously in the background, ensuring seamless multitasking. This powerful combination guarantees lightning-fast app launches, smooth operation, and ample space for all your apps, games, and media. Whether you're gaming, streaming, or juggling multiple tasks, the realme P1 Speed 5G provides a flawless experience across the board.
