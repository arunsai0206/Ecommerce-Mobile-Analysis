    AI Wallpaper Generation

    With sky-rocketing AnTuTu scores, the MediaTek Dimensity 8200 Ultimate 5G Processor is sure to leave you with a smooth, speedy, and powerful smartphone experience. With the ultra-efficient 4nm architecture and clock speed of up to 3.1 GHz, its strong CPU and GPU enable longer gameplay and higher frame rates without compromising performance or consuming more power.

    Up to 24 GB LPDDR5X Expandable RAM

    RAM capacity for the Infinix ZERO 40 5G is 12 GB, with the latest LPDDR5X RAM type. Our ground-breaking MemFusion function allows you to add up to 12 GB extra RAM to the 12 GB that comes installed. With the help of this function, you can carry up to 24GB of RAM in your palm. This also solves all your multi-tasking woes!

    Massive 256 GB/512 GB UFS 3.1 Internal Storage

    The Infinix Zero 40 5G comes with UFS 3.1 256 GB/ 512 GB of internal storage. You can save a large number of images, videos, and apps. So, whether it's your favorite memory or your entire app collection, there's plenty of storage for everything.

    GoPro Ready with GoPro Mode

    Seamlessly connect your Infinix Zero 40 5G with your GoPro device and control it from your phone via the GoPro Quik app and capture breathtaking shots.

    The Infinix Zero 40 5G has a large 5000mAh battery- so the phone does not leave your side at those critical moments. The phone also supports 20 W Wireless Charging and boasts of additional features like bypass charging and TUV Rheinland certification.

    Immersive Sound Experience with JBL

    The Infinix Zero 40 5G boasts dual Hi-Res DTS speakers that ensure an immersive auditory experience, every time you stream content on your phone. Sound by JBL enhances the overall quality of your aural experience.

    Experience Convenience with NFC and IR Control

    The Infinix Zero 40 5G features NFC and Smart IR technology. NFC allows you to make contactless payments and connect to other NFC-enabled devices. With the Welife app, control your home appliances via Smart IR Control. These add a layer of ease to your daily life, making operations like payments and home control simpler and faster.

    The Latest XOS 14.5 Based on Android 14
