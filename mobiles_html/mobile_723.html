    Product Description
    Up to 16 GB RAM + 128 GB ROM

    Spark 30C comes with up to 16 GB RAM, complemented by Memory Fusion technology that extends the virtual RAM, providing superior performance during intensive tasks. With up to 128GB of internal storage, users have ample space for apps, photos, and files.

    6 nm D6300 5G Powerful Processor

    Spark 30C is equipped with the 6nm D6300 5G processor, providing robust performance and excellent power efficiency. It enables smooth multitasking, high-speed 5G connectivity, and a seamless experience while running demanding apps or gaming.

    120 Hz Smooth Display

    Spark 30C boasts a 120 Hz smooth display, providing fluid visuals and reducing motion blur. This ensures users enjoy smooth scrolling, gaming, and video playback, delivering a visually pleasing experience

    48 MP Sony IMX582 AI Camera

    Spark 30C features a 48 MP Sony IMX582 AI-powered camera, ensuring users capture high-resolution, detailed photos. The AI intelligently adjusts settings for optimal lighting and clarity, enhancing every shot, whether in bright or low-light conditions

    5000 mAh Battery with 18 W Charging Support

    Spark 30C is powered by a large 5000 mAh battery, ensuring long-lasting usage on a single charge. The 18 W fast charging support enables users to quickly recharge the device, reducing downtime

    Dual Stereo Speaker with Dolby Atmos

    Spark 30C features dual stereo speakers enhanced by Dolby Atmos technology, offering an immersive and crystal-clear audio experience, making it perfect for music lovers and media enthusiasts

    4+ Years of Lag-free Experience

    Spark 30C is designed to deliver over four years of lag-free performance. This ensures users enjoy consistent speed and responsiveness without the typical slowdown seen in many devices over time

    All-directional NFC and IR Remote

    Spark 30C supports all-directional NFC, allowing users to perform contactless payments and data transfers from multiple angles. This feature adds convenience for quick and secure transactions in stores or during data sharing. In addition, it includes an IR remote, allowing users to control household appliances like TVs or air conditioners, and other smart devices.

    IP54 Splash and Dust Resistant

    Spark 30C is rated IP54, meaning it’s resistant to dust and splashes of water. This adds an extra layer of durability, making the phone more resilient to everyday mishaps like accidental spills or light rain
