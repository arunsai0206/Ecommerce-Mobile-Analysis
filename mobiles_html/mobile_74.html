    • Description
    The iQO Z9X is a 5G smartphone featuring a 4nm Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 processor with a 560K+ AnTuTu score for a smooth, lag-free experience. It has a 6000mAh battery with 44W FlashCharge, offering up to 10 hours of use with just 30 minutes of charging and a two-day battery life on a single charge.     Product Description
    Great Design

    This smartphone comes with a hybrid SIM slot for multiple options. It has a slim design that can easily fit into your hand. Furthermore, it is designed to be waterproof and dust-proof, making it easier to take care of this phone. Moreover, the phone features camera cut-outs along with the flash, in a rectangular shape with rounded edges that offer an elegant look.

    High Storage Capacity

    The phone has a RAM of 6 GB, and it has an internal storage of 128 GB with an expandable storage of up to 1 TB. It supports microSDXC memory card type and features a hybrid slot for memory cards. With such a high storage option available in this phone, you will be able to store photos, videos, movies, and a lot more, making it convenient for you to access these files as and when required.

    Multiple Camera Lenses

    The primary camera comes with a dual camera lens of 50 megapixels and 2 megapixels. You can use this camera in night mode, portrait mode, for videos, photos, panorama shots, slow-motion footage, live photos, time-lapse, and more. Along with the primary camera setup, this phone also features an 8 megapixel secondary camera at the front, which can be used for portrait, photo, live photo, and video. The flash helps with lighting when the visibility is poor.

    Connectivity Advantage

    This phone supports 5G, 4G VoLTE, and 4G LTE networks, and has a Bluetooth 5.1 version for wireless connection. Additionally, it supports a Wi-Fi connection of Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, dual-band version. USB tethering is available on this phone, and it comes with USB connectivity and 3.5 mm audio jack support for earphones. This support makes it convenient for you to connect your phone to other devices for seamless usage.

    Lasting Battery

    When it comes to battery life, this phone has a 6000 mAh battery with a 44 W FlashCharge feature, which offers hours of use with minimal charging time. Moreover, with a single full charge, this phone can continue functioning efficiently for long. With such a high-capacity battery, it becomes easier to work on this phone for hours without having to be concerned about the battery draining out.

    Quality Display

    The display of this phone is also impressive, with a 17.07 cm full HD+ display along with an adaptive refresh rate of 120Hz, 1000 nits high brightness, and 2408x1080 resolution. With this refresh rate and high resolution, it becomes easier to play games, watch movies, and browse through social media as the picture quality is well-enhanced. Moreover, the high brightness feature of the phone contributes to better visibility, both indoors and outdoors.

    Processor for Smooth Experience

    The phone runs on the Funtouch OS 14 operating system, and it is backed by a Qualcomm SM6450 Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 processor, which enables you to enjoy a smooth and lag-free viewing experience. With Adreno 710 GPU and 393 PPI graphics, the phone offers a smooth gaming experience, making the phone more efficient in terms of functionality and performance. Therefore, using this phone for multi-tasking and heavy use will not cause it to run slow.
