    Clean UI with Minimal Pre-installed Apps

    Imagine a smartphone experience free from clutter, where every swipe and tap feels effortless. With the Note 40 Pro 5G, you get just that – a clean user interface devoid of unnecessary apps. Say goodbye to bloatware and hello to a streamlined digital experience.

    The NOTE 40 Pro 5G can prevent the battery and directly charge the motherboard to drastically reduce heat accumulation by 4.5°C during periods of high activity, like gaming or viewing movies, in order to minimise heat buildup and save battery life.

    Wireless Reverse Charging

    Never run out of battery again with the Note 40 Pro's wireless reverse charging feature. Simply flip the switch and power up your other devices on the go.

    With multi-speed fast charging and a high-energy-density 5000 mAh battery, the Note 40 Pro ensures that you're always ready for whatever the day throws at you. And with robust safety features, you can charge with confidence, knowing that your device is protected.

    Infinix X1 Cheetah Chip

    Experience unparalleled performance with the Infinix X1 Cheetah Chip, a revolutionary innovation that takes charging management to the next level. With high-precision power detection and versatile scene support, the X1 Cheetah Chip ensures that your device stays powered up and secure at all times.

    Dual JBL Speakers

    Immerse yourself in crystal-clear sound with the Note 40 Pro's dual JBL stereo speakers. Whether you're watching movies, listening to music, or playing games, the Note 40 Pro delivers an audio experience like no other.

    Powerful Processor

    Powered by a 6 nm MediaTek Dimensity 7020 5G processor, the Note 40 Pro offers unparalleled performance and efficiency. From gaming to multitasking, this processor can handle it all with ease.

    Premium Design

    Step into the future of mobile elegance with the Note 40 Pro's sleek and sophisticated design. Crafted with premium materials and attention to detail, this device is sure to turn heads wherever you go.

    Massive Storage

    With up to 16 GB of RAM and 256 GB of ROM, the Note 40 Pro offers ample storage for all your apps, photos, and media. Say goodbye to storage woes and hello to seamless performance.

    Blazing-fast Charging

    With 45 W multi-speed fast charging, the Note 40 Pro ensures that you spend less time plugged in and more time on the go. And thanks to its 5000 mAh high-energy-density battery, you can enjoy all-day usage on a single charge, no matter how demanding your day gets.
