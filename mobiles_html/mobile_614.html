    Product Description
    Fusion Design

    Feel the opulence with the all-new Fusion Design on the SuperNote. Immerse yourself in the stunning vegan leather texture paired with a stylish colour-blocked pattern that elevates the aesthetics of the Redmi Note series to unparalleled heights. It's not just a smartphone; it's a fashion statement that mirrors your sense of style.

    3D Curved AMOLED Display

    Witness innovation unfolds with the introduction of the first-ever 3D curved display on the Redmi Note series. The 4-sided ultra-thin narrow bezel and a 25-degree curvature not only enhance the visual appeal but are meticulously designed for the perfect hand grip. Immerse yourself in a display that doesn't just meet expectations; it exceeds them.

    In-display Fingerprint Sensor

    Experience the marriage of security and style with Redmi Note's first in-display fingerprint sensor. This cutting-edge technology supports dark light unlocking and heart rate detection, ensuring that unlocking your device is not just secure but seamless. It's a touch of sophistication every time you access your smartphone.

    Sharp 1.5K Resolution

    Elevate your visual experience with the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G's impressive 120Hz 1.5K resolution. Compared to a standard FHD display, this technology provides a much denser pixel-per-inch ratio, resulting in a sharper, visually pleasing experience. Immerse yourself in a world of clarity and vividness like never before.

    Flagship IP68 Protection

    Fearlessly face the elements with the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G's flagship IP68 protection. With 19 key structural components water-proofed, this smartphone reaches pro-grade protection against water and dust. Life's uncertainties are no match for the SuperNote – it's ready for whatever challenges come your way.

    Elegance Meets Endurance

    The curved display on the SuperNote is not just a visual treat; it's fortified with Corning Gorilla(R) Glass Victus(R), the toughest Gorilla(R) Glass yet. This makes the SuperNote twice as scratch-resistant as its predecessor, ensuring enduring elegance that stands the test of time.

    True 200MP With OIS

    The new and improved flagship 200MP ISOCELL HP3 with in-sensor 4X zoom and enhanced computing power gives you a flagship imaging experience, further enhancing the high-resolution experience.

    120 W HyperCharge

    Fuel your day at the speed of thought with the SuperNote's 120 W HyperCharge. Witness a 100% charge in just 19 minutes. But it's not just about speed; it's about intelligent charging. Redmi's own smart charging engine optimises the charging process, ensuring efficiency and longevity for your device.

    Powered by MediaTek Dimensity 7200 Ultra-5G

    Step into the future with the smartphone powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7200 Ultra-5G. Built on the flagship TSMC 4 nm process, the octa-core processor offers an improved performance. The SuperNote is not just powerful; it's cool too, thanks to advanced liquid cooling ensuring smooth operations even in demanding situations.
