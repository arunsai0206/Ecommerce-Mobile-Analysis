    Product Description
    Incredible Design

    Marvel at the near-bezel-less design of the POCO X6 Neo 5G, providing a stunning visual canvas for your digital adventures. The 7.69 mm slim body not only makes it a style statement but also enhances the in-hand feel, letting you effortlessly navigate your device with finesse. Picture yourself effortlessly gliding through your day with a device that complements your lifestyle.

    Vibrant Display

    Experience visuals like never before with the 120 Hz AMOLED display, a rarity in this segment. Your content comes to life with vibrant colours and unparalleled clarity, all protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 5. The display's peak brightness of 1000 nits ensures sunlight won't dim your viewing pleasure. Worried about eye strain? Fear not, as the TUV Rhineland and SGS Low blue light certifications keep your eyes safe, even during prolonged usage.

    Dual Camera Setup

    Unleash your inner photographer with the POCO X6 Neo 5G's dual camera setup. The 108 MP pro-grade main camera captures the essence of every moment with stunning detail. The in-sensor zoom of 3x ensures that even the center of a 108 MP shot is as crisp as a standalone 12 MP photo. Imagine capturing intricate details with a level of clarity that transports you back to the moment.

    Powerful Processor

    Under the hood, the POCO X6 Neo 5G houses the MediaTek Dimensity 6080 processor, a 6 nm 5G powerhouse with a top clock speed of up to 2.4 GHz. It's not just a phone; it's a high-performance companion that effortlessly handles your multitasking needs, ensuring a smooth and responsive experience every time.

    Efficient Storage

    With 256 GB of internal storage and 12 GB of RAM, this phone can easily handle your needs whether you're gaming, streaming, or using numerous apps at once. You also receive 12 GB of virtual RAM, for a total of 24 GB, which lets you multitask without any issues.

    Massive Battery

    Embark on a weekend without worrying about charging, thanks to the POCO X6 Neo 5G's 5000 mAh Weekend Battery. With an impressive standby time of 26.5 days, 29 hours of call time, and 18 hours of YouTube playback at 1080p, this device lets you focus on what matters most. The Type-C charging and 33 W in-the-box charger ensure you're ready to roll whenever needed.

    Exceptional Features

    Immerse yourself in an audio experience like no other with Dolby Atmos-supported single-speaker setup. The sound quality is unparalleled, bringing your favourite content to life. Plus, the inclusion of a 3.5mm headphone jack, a fan-favourite feature, allows you to personalise your audio experience. The IP54 rating ensures your device stays safe from dust and water splashes, making it a durable companion for your daily adventures.
