    Product Description
    Unmatched Design

    The Redmi Note 13 Pro 5G – where Pro meets perfection in design, camera prowess, and incredible technology. Prepare to be captivated by its all-new, double-sided glass body, featuring a super-narrow bezel, available in three elegant shades that redefine smartphone aesthetics.

    Just Zoom Right In

    Now the Pro also gets the pro camera setup with the flagship 200MP ISOCELL HP3. Combined with Xiaomi's high-pixel engine, the flagship camera setup further enhances the high-resolution 200MP experience and in-sensor 4X zoom.

    Powerful Processor

    Under the hood, the Redmi Note 13 Pro 5G proudly houses the world's first Snapdragon(R) 7s Gen 2 5G processor, built on a 4 nm architecture. This octa-core powerhouse not only ensures superior performance but also brings remarkable improvements in graphics rendering, setting a new benchmark for mobile processing.

    A Visual Spectacle

    Experience crystal-clear clarity and immerse yourself in the super smooth and sharp visuals on the 120Hz CrystalRes 1.5K display on the Redmi Note 13 Pro 5G with 94% screen-to-body ratio.

    Highly Durable

    The display is fortified with Corning Gorilla(R) Glass Victus(R), making the SuperNote scratch-resistant. Elegance meets endurance in a display that stands the test of time.

    In-display Fingerprint Sensor

    Unlock a world of possibilities with the in-display fingerprint sensor on the Redmi Note 13 Pro 5G. This cutting-edge technology supports dark light unlocking and heart rate detection, ensuring that one touch is all you need to make things happen. Your smartphone experience just got more seamless and secure.

    67 W Turbo Charge

    Charging your device becomes a breeze with the 67 W Turbo Charge feature. Redmi's revolutionary charging technology not only juices up your smartphone at supersonic speeds, providing a full day's charge in just 19 minutes but is also backed by Redmi's smart charging engine. This engine optimises your charging needs, ensuring longevity and efficiency in every charge.

    Exceptional Features

    Delight in fan-favourite features that enhance your overall smartphone experience. The Redmi Note 13 Pro 5G boasts dual stereo speakers for immersive audio, a 3.5 mm headphone jack for versatile connectivity, an X-axis motor for enhanced haptics, and an IR blaster for added convenience. Moreover, it is IP54-rated, ensuring a note-worthy experience that stands up to the rigours of daily use.
