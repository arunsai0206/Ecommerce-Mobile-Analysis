    Product Description
    Premium Starlight Design: Inspired by the Cosmos

    The Redmi 14C 5G features a Premium Starlight Design that’s both elegant and modern. The glass back editions create a stunning visual appeal, inspired by the brilliance and beauty of the cosmos. With an all-new camera ring and a stylish flat frame, this smartphone not only offers an extraordinary look but also feels incredibly comfortable in hand. The 8.2 mm slim body adds to its sleek design, making it perfect for anyone who appreciates both beauty and functionality in their device.

    5G Entertainer: Your Perfect Entertainment Companion

    Redmi 14C 5G redefines entertainment with its seamless performance. With Lag-free Video Calling, Smooth Livestreaming, Fast Downloads, and Uninterrupted Gaming, the Redmi 14C ensures you’re always connected and engaged in high-quality entertainment. Whether you’re streaming content or battling it out in your favourite games, this phone delivers flawless performance without skipping a beat, offering you the best 5G experience.

    Efficient 4nm Processor

    At the heart of the Redmi 14C is the Super-Fast Snapdragon 4 Gen 2 5G Processor, built on a 4nm architecture for unparalleled power and battery efficiency. The processor’s remarkable performance delivers an AnTuTu score of 4,50,000+, ensuring smooth multitasking, faster app launches, and an overall enhanced smartphone experience. Say goodbye to lag and hello to performance that keeps up with your lifestyle.

    120 Hz Display

    Take your entertainment to new heights with the 120 Hz Display of the Redmi 14C. This smooth, fast refresh rate allows you to enjoy everything from gaming to video watching with crystal-clear clarity and seamless transitions. The biggest and smoothest display on a 5G phone makes every swipe, tap, and scroll feel incredibly responsive, immersing you in an exceptional viewing experience like never before.

    Eye-safe Display

    The Redmi 14C ensures long-lasting comfort during extended use with its Eye-safe Display. Featuring Triple TUV certifications, this display reduces eye strain, even in dim lighting. Whether you’re scrolling through social media or watching your favourite videos, you can enjoy hours of use without worrying about your eyes.

    Massive 5160 mAh Battery

    With the massive 5160 mAh Battery, the Redmi 14C ensures you stay connected, entertained, and productive all day long. Paired with 18W Fast Charging and a 33 W Charger-in-Box, you’ll spend less time waiting for your phone to charge and more time enjoying what matters. Whether you’re gaming or working, this phone has enough juice to last you through long hours of use.

    Unlock More Speed and Storage

    The Redmi 14C is designed to handle everything you need with ease. With 6 GB RAM + 6 GB Virtual RAM, this smartphone ensures smooth performance and seamless multitasking. With up to 128GB storage and the ability to expand up to 1 TB, you have more than enough space for all your apps, photos, videos, and more. Never worry about running out of space again.

    50 MP Dual Camera

    Capture every moment in stunning detail with the 50 MP Dual Camera system of the Redmi 14C. Featuring an f/1.8 sensor and 4-in-1 pixel binning, it delivers sharper, brighter, and more vibrant photos with ease. Whether it's daytime shots or low-light photography, every image is crisp and clear.

    8 MP Selfie Camera

    The 8 MP Selfie Camera ensures you look your best with portrait and beautify modes, perfect for every selfie.
