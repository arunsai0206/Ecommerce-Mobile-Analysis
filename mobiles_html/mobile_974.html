    Product Description
    Created to Perform

    Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 860, the POCO X3 Pro is created to deliver impressive performance. Furthermore, to boost its gaming performance, it has the Adreno 640.

    Smooth Visuals

    The 16.94-cm (6.67) FHD+ display of this smartphone features up to 120 Hz Refresh rate for smooth visuals. It also has up to 240 Hz touch sampling rate for a fast and high responsive gaming experience. Furthermore, the smart display adjusts the flames as per the content type to save battery life and to prevent random screen tearing.

    Impressive File Transfer Speed

    This smartphone comes with a UFS 3.1 storage solution which is fast and ensures that your games and apps are loaded quickly without lag.

    Impressive Cameras

    The POCO X3 Pro features a 48 MP main camera, 8 MP ultra-wide camera, and 2 MP macro and depth cameras. Powered by the Computer Vision-ISP (CV-ISP) on the Snapdragon 860, this camera system is enhanced by various AI features, automatic detection of objects in images, and quick parameter adjustments for impressive photos.

    Charges Quickly

    This smartphone comes with a 5160 mAh battery to ensure that you can stay connected all the long without exhausting the battery life. Also, the 33 W fast charging quickly charges the battery, so you will not have to worry about charging the battery hours before heading out. Also, its USB- PD support ensures that you do not have to carry the charger everywhere.
