    Product Description
    Metal Meets Glass, Awesome

    Meet the Galaxy A55 5G. The simple yet improved Galaxy A series comes in an iconic design featuring a linear camera layout in a metal flat frame design for an easy and intuitive grip. Plus, it’s built with Corning Gorilla Glass Victus+ making it tougher and more damage-resistant.

    Awesome Photos Even in Low Light

    The improved triple cameras absorb more light for bright, sharp, and accurate colors with low noise at night to give you awesome Nightography.

    50 MP of Vibrant Colours and Details

    Snap crisp, detailed shots with the 50MP high-resolution Wide-angle camera. Catch tiny details and dynamic colors for a new visual experience. Perfect for capturing all your share-worthy moments.

    Capture Your Best Side

    The 32 MP Selfie camera allows you to capture high-quality selfies with options to choose warm or natural tones. Utilize Portrait Mode to elevate your pictures, sharpening focus on the subject with precise bokeh and background blur effects.

    Super HDR Videos Even Under Challenging Lighting Conditions

    Capture the world in Super HDR with videos that shine in different lighting conditions, thanks to optimized color and contrast. Record precise and smooth motion with No Shake Cam (OIS) and VDIS stabilization in 4K.

    Edit Photos in a Tap

    Take your photos to the next level with Edit Suggestion at the touch of a button. Mashup elements from various photos in a fun way with Image Clipper.

    Samsung Knox Vault

    Samsung Knox Vault is like a safe within a safe, physically isolating PINS, passwords and other private info so hackers can’t gain access.

    Unleash The Octa-core Power

    Take advantage of the new 4nm Octa-core processor and effortlessly multi-task, game, and stream. Paired with 5G capabilities and internal memory options of 8GB or 12GB, improved GPU, NPU, and a 70% larger cooling system, this phone is an absolute performance beast. Internal storage of 128GB or 256GB and supports a microSD card slot up to 1TB.

    The Immersive and Clear Display

    The 16.83 cm FHD+ Super AMOLED display with 1000 nits brightness provides an immersive viewing experience. Vision Booster enhances visibility by optimizing color and contrast under the sun. This vivid display comes with a refresh rate of up to 120Hz which makes it super responsive.

    Empower Your Day with up to 2-day Battery

    Boundless power is at your fingertips with our 5000mAh (typical) Super Fast Charging battery. Stay connected longer, charge faster, and live life on your terms.

    Durable Against Dust and Water

    IP67 rated protection against dust and water resistance isn't just a feature. It can be your device's shield against the elements. Stay connected, capture memories, and explore with confidence, knowing your device is designed to be safeguarded against dust and accidental water spills.

    OS and Security Update

    OS updates and security maintenance come standard with your Galaxy A55 5G. You'll get up to 4 generations of OS upgrades and up to 5 years of security updates to keep your phone and personal data in good hands.

    Smart Switch

    Easily transfer data to your new Samsung Galaxy from different devices.

    Quick Share

    Sharing files is a breeze with Quick Share. It’s fast, easy, and secure.

    One UI

    One UI 6 lets you run things effortlessly just the way you like it. It’s all about personalizing your everyday experiences.
