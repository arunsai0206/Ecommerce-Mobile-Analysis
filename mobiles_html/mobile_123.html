    Product Description
    Eye-friendly 120 Hz Curved Display

    Experience unparalleled visual brilliance with the realme P2 Pro’s 120Hz Curved AMOLED Display. This screen boasts a bright and eye-friendly technology, ensuring vibrant colours and deep contrasts for an immersive viewing experience. This display features a high dynamic photo technology, it delivers stunning clarity and lifelike details, making every image and video come alive with remarkable precision and brightness. Whether you’re gaming, streaming, or browsing, enjoy a display that sets a new standard for excellence.

    Snapdragon Chipset with 680K+ AnTuTu

    Equipped with the cutting-edge Snapdragon 7s Gen 2, built on a 4 nm high-efficiency process, this beast features an octa-core architecture clocked up to 2.4 GHz. With an Antutu score soaring past 680,000, expect lightning-fast app launches, minimal waiting times, and seamless performance even under heavy loads. Whether you're gaming or multitasking, enjoy stable frame rates and uninterrupted smoothness that keep you ahead of the game.

    80 W Fast Charging with 5200 mAh Battery

    The realme P2 Pro takes charging performance to the next level with its innovative battery technology. By combining high energy density graphite with fast-charging graphite, it significantly enhances both the overall charging efficiency and energy density of the cell. Equipped with the segment's only 80 W Fast Charging and a robust 5200 mAh battery, it offers faster charging and extended battery life. This durable battery remains rejuvenated even after four years of use, providing you with a larger capacity and longer-lasting power. Experience higher charging power and quicker recharge times, ensuring your device is always ready when you are.

    Avian Design

    Avian Design brings the power of nature to your hands. An advanced process creates high-speed design effects that activate under different lighting conditions, reflecting the agility and grace of a bird in flight. Every detail is crafted to keep you soaring, with performance and design in perfect harmony.

    4500 mm² VC Cooling System

    The realme P2 Pro features the segment's largest 4500 mm² VC Stainless Steel Cooling System, designed to deliver superior thermal management. Its advanced 9-layer composite heat sink structure significantly expands the heat dissipation area, covering major heat sources on the motherboard. This results in enhanced cooling efficiency and more stable performance during intense gaming or multitasking. With an increased VC area, the realme P2 Pro ensures your device stays cool and responsive, even under the most demanding conditions.

    Sony LYT-600 OIS Camera

    Capture brilliance day or night with the realme P2 Pro’s Sony LYT-600 OIS Camera. Featuring a 1/2" large sensor and AI HyperRAW Algorithm, it takes in more light for stunning night photography. With 2x optical zoom and OIS, your shots stay sharp and blur-free, even in low light. Whether it’s high dynamic range in daylight or crystal-clear night shots, this camera delivers unmatched clarity every time
