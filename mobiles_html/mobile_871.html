    Product Description
    1.5K 3D Curved AMOLED Display

    Experience unparalleled clarity with POCO X7's 1.5K resolution display, delivering razor-sharp visuals that bring every image, video, and text to life. The innovative 3D Curved AMOLED design not only enhances viewing but also provides a premium, ergonomic feel that’s unmatched in its segment.

    Bright AMOLED Display - 3000nits

    Whether under direct sunlight or low-light conditions, the POCO X7's AMOLED display shines with a stunning 3000nits brightness, ensuring visibility in any environment. Experience vivid colors and rich contrasts like never before.

    Wet Touch Display 2.0

    Navigate seamlessly even in challenging scenarios with POCO X7's Wet Touch Display 2.0. Whether you're caught in the rain or have oil-stained fingers, the optimized touch recognition ensures precision and avoids accidental triggers.

    Durable Curved Display Phone

    With 17 key components protected under IP66 + IP68 standards and supporting IP69, POCO X7 is built to endure. From splashes to dust, its durable construction ensures peace of mind, making it the toughest curved display phone in its category.

    Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2

    Stay worry-free with Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 protection on the POCO X7. Designed to withstand drops, scratches, and impacts, this cutting-edge glass ensures your device remains pristine.

    MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultra

    Powered by the latest TSMC 4nm process, the MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultra offers unmatched efficiency and performance. With flagship-grade ISP technology and enhanced GPU architecture, the POCO X7 ensures smoother gaming, better multitasking, and improved image quality.

    50 MP Sony LYT 600 Primary Camera & 8 MP Ultra-Wide Camera

    The POCO X7’s 50 MP Sony LYT-600 primary camera, with OIS and EIS, captures stunningly detailed photos, even in low light, thanks to its f1.5 large aperture. Advanced features like AI Portrait Mode, Ultra Clear Zoom, and MFNR ensure sharp, vibrant images in any setting. Complemented by an 8MP ultra-wide lens with a 120° field of view and a 20MP AI-enhanced selfie camera, the POCO X7 delivers a versatile and professional photography experience

    AI-Enhanced Gallery & Imaging

    Leverage AI-powered tools like AI Portrait Mode, AI Selfie Zoom, and AI Night Mode to capture and edit photos with precision. It comes with AI Erase Pro & AI Image Expansion which will be available in future updates. The gallery's intelligent features ensure every shot looks professional and vibrant.
