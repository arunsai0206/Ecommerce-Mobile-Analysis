    Product Description
    Incredible Camera

    Take your photography to the flagship level with the Phone (3a) Pro's three cameras system. Boasting a large, light-absorbing 50 MP sensor with the largest full well capacity in its class, a flagship 50 MP periscope sensor with up to 60X ultra zoom, a 50 MP front camera for superstar selfies, and a 120º ultra-wide field of view. All four sensors support Ultra HDR photo output and the main and front support 4K video recording.

    Hello, Snapdragon

    Experience unmatched power and efficiency with the latest Snapdragon chipset, crafted on cutting-edge TSMC 4nm technology. Equipped with the upgraded Qualcomm Kryo CPU architecture and Qualcomm Adreno 810 GPU, it delivers an 8% boost in power efficiency compared to the Phone (2a), offering up to 0.5 hours of extra battery life. Performance takes a leap forward with a 33% increase in CPU power and an 11% GPU upgrade over the Phone (2a), achieving an impressive AnTuTu score of 825,564. From seamless multitasking to ultra-smooth mobile gaming, this chipset ensures every experience is fast, responsive, and effortless.

    High-end Visuals

    Phone (3a) Pro has a flexible FHD+ 17.19 cm (6.77) AMOLED display that can deliver stunningly accurate colour reproduction of 1.07 billion colours. Boasting a peak brightness of 3000 nits, which is our brightest screen, ensuring superb visibility even in direct sunlight. It also guarantees the display of Ultra HDR photos, utilizing the highest brightness to showcase highlights in images, such as blue skies, lights, and more.

    Android Supremacy

    Nothing OS delivers a fast and smooth Android experience rooted in utility and functional customization, streamlining your smartphone use for a more intuitive experience. Customize your navigation by selecting a monochromatic theme to reduce distractions or removing app labels for a cleaner look. Our expanding widget library breathes new life into your lock and home screens, while we continuously optimize software for Phone (3a) and Phone (3a) Pro to ensure the fastest, most fluid experience.

    The Ultimate Hub

    Say goodbye to the hassle of juggling multiple apps for your important notes, images, and inspirations. With the new Essential Key, you can effortlessly grab photos, record your screen, and capture your voice—all in one place. Everything you capture is stored in your Essential Space, an AI-powered sanctuary that organizes your notes, ideas, and inspirations. The system recognizes content, transcribes text and audio, and describes images, automatically sorting and cataloging everything for easy access. Plus, you can create custom groups for your projects and ideas, making it simpler than ever to keep your digital life organized and ready for action. And ask, listen and learn from ChatGPT on-the-go. Get the answers you need faster, and at your fingertips. Text, voice or image search right from your home screen.

    Charge Fast

    The Phone (3a) Pro can last up to two days, thanks to its expansive 5000 mAh battery. For those who don’t have time to wait, the Phone (3a) Pro features rapid 50W Fast Charging, allowing it to reach 50% battery in just about 19 minutes—nearly 10% faster than the Phone (2a). Thanks to our advanced battery health management system, the battery is designed to retain over 90% of its full capacity even after 1,200 charging cycles. This means that if you fully charge the battery from 0% to 100% once a day, it will take approximately 3 years and 4 months (1,200 days) before the battery's capacity drops to 90%, ensuring long-lasting performance for years of daily use.
