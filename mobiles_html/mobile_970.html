    Product Description
    50 MP Dual Camera with Quad-LED Ring Flash

    Capture the world in vivid detail with the 50 MP Dual AI Camera featuring a Quad-LED Ring Flash. This advanced camera system doesn't just take pictures; it tells a story. The smart AI Camera recognises scenes and conditions, ensuring that each photo you click is a masterpiece. Whether it's a breathtaking landscape or a cherished moment, the Smart 8's camera elevates your photography game to new heights.

    90 Hz Punch-hole Display

    Immerse yourself in the brilliance of the 16.76 cm (6.6) HD+ Punch-Hole Display that brings content to life like never before. With a high refresh rate of 90 Hz, every swipe, scroll, and video becomes a seamless visual experience. Say goodbye to lag and hello to a display that sets new standards for budget smartphones.

    Magic Ring

    The Infinix Smart 8 introduces the Magic Ring, a game-changer in user convenience. From Face Unlock to Background Call, Charging Animation, Charge completion reminder, and Low Battery Reminder – the Magic Ring is your gateway to a smarter, more intuitive smartphone experience. It's not just a phone; it's a companion that understands your needs.

    Premium Design

    Who says budget means compromising on aesthetics? The Smart 8 defies expectations with its premium design featuring Timber Texture. It not only looks sophisticated but also feels stunning in your hands. Elevate your style without breaking the bank, because the Smart 8 proves that budget smartphones can be as classy as their premium counterparts.

    Enhanced Security

    Your privacy matters and the Smart 8 takes it seriously. Enjoy enhanced security with the side-mounted fingerprint sensor, ensuring quick and secure access to your device. Add to that the fast face unlock feature, and you have a smartphone that prioritises your safety without sacrificing convenience.

    Up to 8 GB RAM and 64 GB ROM

    Unleash the power of multitasking with up to 8 GB of RAM (4 GB + 4 GB Virtual) and a spacious 64 GB of storage. The Smart 8 not only stores everything you need but also runs apps seamlessly, thanks to its robust configuration. Experience a smooth and responsive performance that adapts to your every command.

    5000 mAh Big Battery

    Power through your day and beyond with the mighty 5000 mAh battery on the Infinix Smart 8. Never worry about running out of juice again, and when it's time to recharge, the Type-C charging ensures a quick and hassle-free experience.

    Smooth Performance

    Whether you're browsing, streaming, or switching between apps, the Smart 8 keeps up with you effortlessly. The powerful Octa-core processor, featuring the MediaTek Helio G36, ensures a smooth performance that matches your dynamic lifestyle.

    Stunning Selfies

    Elevate your social media presence with the Smart 8's 8MP Selfie Camera featuring Front Flash. Capture stunning selfies at any time of the day, and let your personality shine through with sharp and crisp images.

    Triple Card Slot

    Experience unparalleled convenience with the Smart 8's Triple Card Slot. Support for 2 4G SIM cards and a micro SD card up to 1TB means you can stay connected and expand your storage without compromise.

    DTS Processing Sound

    Immerse yourself in an unparalleled audio experience with DTS processing sound on the Smart 8. Whether you're gaming, streaming, or listening to music, the sound quality is nothing short of cinematic.

    Loaded with Features

    The features don't stop with the Infinix Smart 8. Running on the latest XOS 13, based on Android 13GO, this smartphone comes with a host of cool additions. From DTS speaker Eye Care to AI Gallery, OTG Support, and more – the Smart 8 is not just a phone; it's a pocket-sized powerhouse.
