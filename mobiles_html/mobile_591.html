    Sony Camera

    Meet the Sony LYT-600, a 50MP powerhouse camera that’s a game-changer in smartphone photography.     Enhance your visuals like never before with AI-powered resolution and clarity upgrades! With this unique attribute, we set a new standard in practicality and playability, outshining all competitors.

    World's Top Artist Monet Design

    realme has transformed Monet's iconic "Haystacks" and "Water Lilies" into handheld masterpieces. Inspired by Monet’s art, the smartphones feature elegant gold and purple hues, evoking golden haystacks and blooming water lilies, creating a sense of comfort and vitality.     120 Hz Curved Vision Display

    Experience stunning visuals on a 6.7-inch OLED display, featuring Pro-XDR technology that optimizes photo effects by restoring the dynamic range and light and dark details of HDR photos. Enjoy superior eye comfort with 2160Hz PWM dimming, which adjusts for low-brightness environments to maintain accurate color and eliminate low-brightness flashing. The 20,000-level automatic brightness adjustment ensures precise and smooth transitions, while AI Eye Protection adapts to your fatigue levels, adjusting color temperature and brightness for optimal comfort. Rainwater Smart Touch improves touch accuracy even in wet conditions, making this display a perfect blend of innovation and practicality.

    Witness the largest storage for the Number Pro Series, featuring up to 12 GB + 12 GB Dynamic RAM for seamless multitasking, and up to 512GB storage, enough to store 1,940 episodes, 120,000 songs, or 120,000 photos.

    Biggest Battery for Number Pro Series

    Charge rapidly with 45W SUPERVOOC charging and enjoy extended usage with the robust 5200mAh battery. Get optimal charging efficiency and top-tier device safety with AI Smart Charging and intelligent five-core chip protection.
