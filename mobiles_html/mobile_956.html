    Product Description
    Slim and Streamlined Body

    With a thickness of 0.81 cm and 198 g weight, Y29 easily fits into pockets. Stylish design integrating simple aesthetics, this smartphone showcases exceptional taste.

    5500 mAh Battery with 44W FlashCharge

    5500 mAh Battery : Embrace the long-lasting 5500 mAh battery with Battery Saver mode for ultra-long battery life. 44W FlashCharge : Get hours of battery life in just a few minutes.

    Military Grade Shock Resistance

    The Comprehensive Cushioning Structure, paired with a wave-crest phone case, provides the phone with "drop-resistant armor," making it safer to use. The screen is made from more sturdy materials and a raised cover design to enhance drop resistance.

    IP64 Rating

    With IP64 Dust and Water Resistance, the phone is more durable, resilient against harsh weather, and always ready to go further with you.

    Dynamic Light

    A compact, circular Dynamic Light balances the entire camera module design. It supports different colors of flashes in different scenarios, as light and agile as a breath, giving life to the rear camera design.

    Dual Stereo Speaker, 300% Volume

    Volume is boosted to up to 300%, ensuring a clear sound for ringtones, videos, and music even in noisy environments. The Dual Stereo Speaker enhances audio quality with more details for an immersive stereo sound experience, creating a richer atmosphere during music playback.

    50 MP HD Rear Camera

    50 MP HD Rear Camera captures more details, making both landscapes and portraits clearer and more refined. Capture every clear and detailed photo with a 50MP HD Rear Camera. Every shot is perfect and memorable.

    AI Erase

    With AI Erase, you are free to remove whatever you wish from the frame. Be it people or odd objects, nothing stands in the way of you getting that perfect snapshot.

    AI Photo Enhance

    Using advanced AI technology, AI Photo Enhance automatically optimises and enhances photos. It improves facial clarity, restores details like eyes and mouth, and enhances color performance, transforming ordinary photos and unclear old images into high-quality ones to bring beautiful moments back to life.

    AI Night Algorithms

    In low-light or nighttime conditions, Vivo's AI Night Mode algorithms enhance the brightness, vibrancy, and clarity of photos while eliminating blurriness and noise. Whether you're at a party or in a bar, the powerful Night Mode algorithms effortlessly capture high-definition moments in various night scenes, ensuring worry-free photography day and night.

    Multi-style Portrait

    In Portrait mode, simply choose among various portrait styles—special photography skills are unnecessary. You can switch between styles with just one tap, and capture personalised and stylized shots that stand out.

    Stylish Night Filters

    We have developed nine filters in an innovative way specifically for shooting at night. The combination of these personalized styles brings more room for creativity at night, helping you explore and record the beauty at night any way you like.

    120 Hz High-Brightness Display

    With a brightness of up to 1000 nits, the display remains clear and vibrant outdoors even when the sunlight is strong. With a 120 Hz refresh rate, the 16.96 cm (6.68) dotch display offers a wider viewing area and a more immersive experience for everyday use, streaming, and entertainment.
