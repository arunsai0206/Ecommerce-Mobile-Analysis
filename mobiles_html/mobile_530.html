    Product Description
    Unique Gem-Cut Camera Module with Active Halo Lighting

    The gem-cut camera module exudes a unique and stylish aesthetic, with its symmetrical layout and precisely beveled edges, creating a refined, jewel-like elegance. What's more, find the Active Halo Lighting integrated in this one-of-a-kind camera deco. With a classy ivory glow, watch it light up your smartphone experience- whether it's getting notifications, calls, or charging your phone. No more manual countdowns for clicking pictures- simply watch the Active Halo light diminish as per the countdown!

    MediaTek Dimensity 7300 ULTIMATE Processor

    Powered by the World’s 1st MediaTek Dimensity 7300 ULTIMATE Processor, this phone delivers ultra-fast performance, smooth gaming, and seamless multitasking. The dedicated NPU 655 boosts AI performance, making photos sharper, apps smarter, and your phone faster for a smoother experience!

    Military Grade Durability

    The NOTE 50x 5G+ is MIL-STD-810H certified and IP64 rated for ultimate durability; so your phone can handle drops, dust, water, and extreme temperatures with ease. Stay worry-free in any adventure, whether you're hiking, traveling, or just tackling daily mishaps!

    5500 mAh SolidCore Battery and 45 W FastCharge

    The NOTE 50x 5G+ packs a powerful semi-solid state battery with a massive 5500mAh capacity. Also, solve all your charging woes with a Type-C 45W Fast Charger. With All-Round FastCharge 3.0, enjoy features like wired reverse charging, bypass charging, AI Charge Protection, Smart Charge mode, and Low-Temp Charge mode, to optimize your battery and charging speed.

    The Ultimate Gaming Experience!

    Packed with the powerful MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultimate Processor, play your favourite games faster and smoother! XArena ensures you have an efficient gameplay experience, with multiple performance optimization options. A large and vibrant punch-hole display with a 120 Hz refresh rate, along with dual speakers with DTS audio processing elevate your experience to the next level!

    AI that is "Fun"-ctional: One-Tap Infinix AI

    Presenting the next generation of Infinix AI that comprises a lot of fun and productive features. In the mood for some fun with images? Generate wallpapers based on text (or images), or access the AIGC Portrait mode to click AI-generated pictures in real time! With AI Note convert your doodles on the Notepad to share-worthy sketches in a matter of minutes!

    Folax: Your Very Own AI Assistant!

    Folax is here to make your life easy! Want to know what the weather is like, switch on the camera, or just have a friendly conversation, Folax is capable of doing it all and much more. With the capabilities of a powerful AI model, Folax offers a superior AI voice assistant alternative.

    Refreshed XOS 15

    The all-new XOS 15 is based on the latest Android 15 and offers a host of customization options. You can now not only customize the size of the icons but also the shape and the colors! Equipped with several special functions that support multi-tasking (Floating Window, Dynamic Bar), performance (Game Mode), and Security (Theft Alert, Peek Proof), XOS 15 can help make your smartphone experience smooth and convenient.

    50 MP Dual Rear Camera

    The NOTE 50x 5G+ comes with a 50MP ultra-clear dual rear camera with dual LED flash- so your photos are always clean and bright. With over 12 photography modes, and AI features like AI Eraser, ensure that your photography game is on point. Packed with an 8MP Selfie camera, front flash, and screen flash, enjoy clicking selfies like never before!

    Big Memory: Do More, Store More!

    Running out of space won't ever be an issue for you. With a massive 128GB GB of internal storage, the NOTE 50x 5G+ has plenty of space for all of your apps, images, and videos. What's more, the 6GB RAM can be expanded to 12GB, thanks to MemFusion Technology, ensuring you have a smooth experience.

    A Host of Other Features!

    The NOTE 50x 5G+ packs a plethora of useful features. An integrated IR sensor on the camera deco makes your life convenient by doubling up as a remote control. Say no to security compromises with a reliable side-mounted fingerprint sensor and face unlock.
