    5G At Your Fingertips

    Use the Redmi 13C 5G to fully experience the power of a 5G connection.     Star Trail Design

    The Redmi 13C 5G's breathtaking Star Trail Design, stand out from the crowd. You will always be the centre of attention thanks to its striking design. When light falls on the phone, the distinctive design comes to life and gives the impression of star trails, which adds a touch of refinement and elegance. With a phone that looks as good as it works, you can move quickly and stylishly.

    50 MP AI Camera with Classic Film Filters

    Enjoy every moment in breathtaking detail thanks to the Redmi 13C 5G's 50 MP AI Camera. This strong camera makes sure that your pictures are crisp and colourful day or night. Your picture is improved by the AI's ability to automatically modify settings to suit varying lighting situations. You may also give your images a vintage feel with the classic film filters, which give you the creative control to guide your own memories.

    90 Hz Smooth 17.11 cm (6.74) Display

    Enjoy your favourite material to the fullest on the Redmi 13C 5G's expansive 17.11 cm (6.74) display. This large screen makes for an engrossing visual experience, making it ideal for watching films, TV series, and videos. Every game you play will run smoothly at a refresh rate of 90 Hz, giving you a competitive advantage with quicker, more responsive gaming. Take enjoyment in vibrant images and seamless transitions that enhance every phone connection.

    Win at Everything with Dimensity 6100+

    The Dimensity 6100+ CPU, which powers the Redmi 13C 5G, offers lightning-fast performance. This CPU manages everything with ease, whether you're multitasking between applications, browsing social media, or playing graphics-intensive games. Take advantage of lag-free, efficient performance that enables you to do more. With the Dimensity 6100+, your phone will always be prepared to keep up with your hectic schedule.

    Up to 16 GB RAM and 256 GB UFS 2.2 Storage

    With the Redmi 13C 5G, you never have to worry about running out of space. Up to 16 GB of RAM makes multitasking fluid and makes it easy to switch between programmes. You have plenty of room on the 256 GB UFS 2.2 storage to keep all of your files, including movies and memories. Additionally, you can increase storage up to 1 TB via the dedicated SIM card, so you'll always have enough room for your digital life.

    Be 5G Fast and Look Good All Day Long

    The Redmi 13C 5G boasts endurance in addition to quickness. With a 5000 mAh battery, you can continue through the day without worrying about needing to recharge frequently since you will always be charged up. With the 18 W rapid charging (10 W charger that comes in the package), you can refuel your phone fast and resume your favourite activities. Maintain your connection and take full most of your smartphone experience.

    Quick Unlock Fingerprint Scanner

You may quickly and securely unlock your phone with a single touch, protecting your sensitive data and providing you with peace of mind.
