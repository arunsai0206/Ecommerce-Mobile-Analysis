    Product Description
    Fast Curved Phone

    Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 9200+ chipset, this 4 m chipset features over 17 billion transistors and advanced APU Fusion technology to deliver unmatched power and efficiency. It is like a supercar equipped with a V10 engine, ensuring extraordinary performance. Antutu Score of 1,600K+.

    50 MP Sony IMX921 OIS Rear Camera

    Experience pro-level photography with the 50 MP Sony IMX921 camera sensor. This 1/1.56 large sensor, combined with an f/1.88 aperture, captures incredible detail and vibrant colors, even in low light. Optical image stabilization can effectively reduce the blur caused by shaky hands and increase the amount of light intake by 2.8 times. Thus, photos taken at night still depict clear details.

    IP68 Dust and Water Resistance

    T3 Ultra has set a benchmark for combining slimness with water resistance. Whether in the rain or at the beach, T3 Ultra's IP68 rating ensures it can withstand submersion in up to 1.5 meters of fresh water for 30 minutes.

    1.5K AMOLED Display

    1.5K ultra clear AMOLED display and 3D curved screen, coupled with a 120 Hz refresh rate and 452 PPI, provide you with an immersive clear, smooth, and detail-rich visual experience, forming the basis of your love for this flagship display. T3 Ultra's 3D AMOLED curved display also achieves a peak brightness of 4500 nits, ensuring clarity even under direct sunlight.

    50 MP AF Group Selfie Camera

    Snap stunning group selfies with a flagship level 50 MP group selfie camera. The 92° ultra-wide angle ensures everyone fits in the frame. Day or night, your selfies will be clear and vibrant, thanks to advanced Al enhancements.

    5500 mAh Battery

    Stay powered throughout your day: with the massive 5500 mAh battery. Whether you're working, playing, or streaming, you'll enjoy longer use without constantly reaching for your charger.

    80 W FlashCharge

    The 80 W FlashCharge powers up T3 Ultra from 0% to 100% in 56 minutes. With Zero-power Superfast Startup, your phone lights up in just 3 seconds from zero battery', ensuring you're always ready to go.

    4K HD Ultra Stable Video

    Both front and rear main cameras support 4K at 60 FPS resolution video recording, bringing you a clearer video shooting experience and more post-production video editing options. Enjoy high-res video calls and create pro-level vlogs with ease.

    AI Erase

    With T3 Ultra's powerful AI Erase feature, you can easily remove passersby, obstacles, and other unwanted elements from your photos by simply circling or smudging them with your fingers. It is not only efficient and fast, but also preserves great details, helping you effortlessly restore beautiful moments with just one tap.

    AI Photo Enhance

    AI Photo Enhance uses advanced AI technology to automatically optimize and enhance photos. Al scene recognition and real-time adjustments ensure your photos always look their best, capturing every moment perfectly. It can also turn those unsatisfactory photos and old, unclear photos into high-quality ones, reproducing those beautiful moments.

    Studio-quality Aura Light

    Capture perfect portraits with the studio-quality Aura Light, featuring a nanoscale light guide that's 9 times larger and 45 times softer than typical flash, for natural, even lighting. Vivo's exclusive smart Aura Light also provides visual notifications for incoming calls, messages, and photo countdowns. This multifunctional light can add brilliance to your daily life.
