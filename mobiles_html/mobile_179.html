    Product Description
    Stunning Display

    Featuring a stunning full HD+ display, this Redmi 13 5G mobile offers an immersive viewing experience. Its 120Hz AdaptiveSync technology ensures smooth scrolling and responsive touch interactions. With Corning Gorilla Glass 3 protection, its display remains durable against scratches and minor impacts. Its low blue light and flicker-free technology contribute to reduced eye strain, making extended use comfortable. Designed to be TUV Circadian Friendly, it caters to user well-being. Its wet finger touch display capability allows for easy navigation even in humid conditions.

    Smart Performance

    Boasting powerful performance, this Redmi 13 5G is equipped with the Qualcomm Snapdragon 4 Gen 2 AE processor, featuring an octa-core configuration that reaches speeds of up to 2.3GHz. With up to 8 GB of RAM and up to 128 GB of internal storage, it ensures smooth multitasking and ample space for your apps, photos, and files. Running on Android 14 with Xiaomi HyperOS, this device provides an intuitive interface and enhanced functionalities. Also, it offers essential calling features, including call wait/hold, conference call, video call support, calls divert, calls timer, speakerphone, and speed dialling, making it a versatile choice for efficient and convenient communication in any situation.

    Impressive Camera

    Sporting an impressive camera setup, this Redmi 13 5G includes a 108 MP rear camera with a f/1.75 dual lens configuration. Its 3X in-sensor zoom feature enhances photography capabilities, allowing for detailed close-up shots. With options like classic film filters, portrait mode, and night mode, capturing diverse images becomes enjoyable. The HDR and 108 MP modes ensure vibrant and detailed photographs in various lighting conditions. Additionally, its 13MP selfie camera enables high-quality self-portraits, catering to the needs of photography enthusiasts.

    Extensive Support

    Featuring a long-lasting 5030 mAh battery, this Redmi 13 5G supports extended usage without frequent charging. The included 33 W fast charger ensures quick power replenishment, minimising downtime. With USB Type-C connectivity, this device offers compatibility with various accessories. Its dual SIM and hybrid SIM slot provide flexibility for users, allowing for dual connectivity options. Being OTG compatible, this device supports external storage solutions. A 3.5 mm headphone jack adds convenience for audio connectivity, while NFC and GPS support enhance its functionality. Also, it is equipped with an ambient light sensor, e-compass, accelerometer, vibration motor, IR blaster, side fingerprint sensor, and supports MMS, SMS, nano-sim, 5G and 4G Volte networks, GPRS, Bluetooth, WiFi, and GPS.
