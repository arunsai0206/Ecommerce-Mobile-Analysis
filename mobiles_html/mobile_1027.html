    Product Description
    Discover the Pinnacle of Performance

    Presenting the fastest smartphone in its class, the IQOO Z7 Pro 5G is built to take your mobile experience to new heights. The advanced Dimensity 7200 5G CPU, built on the most recent 4 nm energy-efficient technology, is at the core of this powerhouse. With its unparalleled efficiency and lightning-fast speed, this innovative chipset makes gaming, streaming, and multitasking easier than ever.

    Immersive Visual Delight

    Your senses will be captivated by the breathtaking 3D Curved Super-vision Display of the IQOO Z7 Pro 5G. Thanks to its large screen size of 17.22 cm (6.78) and remarkable screen-to-body ratio of 93.3%, this device provides an expansive vision that fully immerses you in every detail. With 1.07 billion colours and a peak local brightness of 1300 nits, the 120 Hz AMOLED FHD+ display brings your content to life with smooth action and brilliant colours. The visual experience is amazing whether you're surfing, playing games, or watching movies.

    Sleek and Stylish Design

    The premium glass finish of the IQOO Z7 Pro 5G combines elegance and sophistication. At just 0.736 centimetres (7.36 mm) thick, it is the thinnest phone in its class. In addition to improving the phone's visual attractiveness, the Premium AG Matte Glass coating offers a pleasant grip that makes it enjoyable to hold and operate.

    Revolutionary Camera System

    With the 64 MP AURA Light OIS Camera, you can capture the world in breathtaking clarity. With features like Super Night Mode and Hybrid Image Stabilisation, this cutting-edge camera technology guarantees clear, crisp images even in low light. In addition to giving your photos a polished look, the Aura Light Portrait and Camera Panning Portrait capabilities allow you to shoot 4K video and store your memories in incredibly high clarity. With the IQOO Z7 Pro 5G, you may improve your photographic abilities and let your imagination run wild.

    Long-lasting Power and Thermal Performance

    With just a few minutes of charge, the fast-charging solution of this smartphone guarantees that your phone will be ready to use in no time, giving you hours to use it. The IQOO Z7 Pro 5G keeps you connected and energised all day long, no matter how busy you are. Gamers, rejoice! The sophisticated Liquid Cooling System of the IQOO Z7 Pro 5G makes it easy to manage long gaming sessions. By effectively dispersing heat, the 30 cm² (3000 mm²) Vapour Chamber ensures peak performance without overheating. Motion Control and an Instant Touch Sampling Rate (TSR) of 1200 Hz provide for a fluid and responsive gaming experience.
