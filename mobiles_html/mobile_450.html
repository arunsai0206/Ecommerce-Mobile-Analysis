    Product Description
    Effortlessly Aesthetic

    The Motorola Edge 50 Ultra isn't just a phone, it's an ergonomic symphony. From the moment you grip its flawlessly symmetrical curves, a sense of harmony flows through every element. It has the FSC-certified real wood finish with a precision-cut aluminium frame. The endless edge display, virtually bezel-free, curves seamlessly into the palm of your hand, a perfect marriage of form and function.

    Bring Your Imagination to Life with AI Magic Canvas

    The Motorola Edge 50 Pro utilizes AI technology to transform the smartphone experience. Transform your imagination into stunning visuals with just a text prompt thanks to Magic Canvas. Experience the future of creativity as Moto Ai crafts unique artwork from your ideas, seamlessly and beautifully.

    Effortlessly Capture with Moto AI

    Capture life's moments on the move, effortlessly. Moto AI empowers your photography with intelligent features that adapt to your every step. Imagine smooth, stabilized videos thanks to Adaptive Stabilization, which uses AI to anticipate your movements. Capture fleeting moments in stunning detail with Adaptive Shutter, as AI predicts subject movement and adjusts the shutter speed for razor-sharp photos. And say goodbye to blurry action shots – Auto Focus Tracking locks onto your subject with AI precision, ensuring they stay perfectly in focus, even as they move.

    Effortlessly Closer with 100X Super Zoom

    The most impressive zoom camera to date brings distant scenes into breathtaking detail. Packed with AI-powered telephoto OIS camera it lets you get 3x closer with crystal-clear 100X AI superzoom. No matter the distance, built-in Optical Image Stabilization ensures steady shots for crisp, clear photos. This versatile powerhouse isn't just for landscapes – it also captures stunning portraits, ensuring you never miss a detail.

    Effortlessly Brilliant Pro Grade AI Camera System

    The Motorola Edge 50 Ultra elevates your mobile photography with a triple-camera system powered by motoAI. The 50MP main sensor has 2.4um Super Ultra Pixels and 1/1.3 captures stunning detail and handles low-light situations exceptionally well. For breathtaking landscapes or group shots, the 122-degree ultrawide lens fits more into the frame while maintaining a high 50 MP resolution. Even selfies get a boost with the impressive 50MP front camera, ensuring clear and detailed self-portraits.

    Pantone Validated Camera and Display for True-to-life colours and skin tones

    The Motorola Edge 50 Ultra has been validated by Pantone, the authority on colour. This means every photo you take bursts with authentic colour, replicating the full range of real-world shades we see around us. But it doesn't stop there. The Motorola Edge 50 Ultra is also Pantone SkinTone Validated, ensuring every picture, every portrait, reflects the beautiful diversity of human skin tones.

    Effortlessly Cinematic 144 Hz 3D Curved Display

    Forget bezels, forget boundaries. The Motorola Edge 50 Ultra invites you on a visual adventure with its stunning, nearly edge-to-edge 17.22 cm (6.7) pOLED display with Gorilla Glass Victus Protection. Dive into a world of sharper details and vibrant colours, thanks to the 144 Hz 3D curved display with 2800 nits of peak brightness and 10-bit billion colour support. Every movie, show, or game comes alive in a billion shades of true-to-life colour with HDR10+ technology. And for the ultimate in smooth performance, the display boasts an unbelievably fast 144 Hz refresh rate.

    Effortlessly Connected with Smart Connect

    Imagine a world where your devices work together seamlessly, like a well-rehearsed orchestra. With Motorola Edge 50 Ultra's Smart Connect, this vision becomes a reality. Effortlessly transition tasks between your phone, tablet, and PC. Share files securely, access mobile apps on your computer, and manage notifications without breaking your flow. Boost your productivity by editing documents, streaming applications, and sharing notes across all your devices.

    Effortlessly Fast with Snapdragon 8S Gen 3

    The Motorola Edge 50 Ultra isn't just powerful, it's a revolution. The heart beats with the all-new Snapdragon 8s Gen 3 mobile platform, pushing performance boundaries with clock speeds reaching a blistering 3.0 GHz. Multitasking becomes effortless, and efficiency soars. Experience the supercharged Qualcomm AI Engine, enabling on-device generative AI that works at breakneck speeds. This phone boasts the fastest LPDDR5X memory on the market and the cutting-edge UFS 4.0 storage, ensuring lightning-fast responsiveness. Not only can you connect to blazing-fast 5G today, but you're also future-proofed for the mind-blowing speeds of tomorrow's WiFi 7 networks.

    Effortlessly Shareable Across Various Devices

    Imagine a world where your phone and PC seamlessly merge into a unified powerhouse. With Motorola Edge 50 Ultra's Smart Connect, that world is here. Simply connect your phone and unleash a symphony of possibilities. Effortlessly share files and photos, transforming the barrier between devices into a bridge of pure convenience. Take video calls to a whole new level, projecting your polished self to the world with your phone's high-resolution camera acting as the perfect webcam.
