    Dazzling Diamond Design

    The Realme 14x 5G smartphone is crafted to impress with its breathtaking diamond-cut design. A perfect harmony of aesthetics and rugged durability, this phone catches the eye while offering lasting resilience. Whether you're taking it to work or out on an adventure, its elegant yet sturdy build ensures it’s always ready for action.

    Massive 6000 mAh Battery + 45 W Fast Charge

    Say goodbye to battery anxiety with the 6000 mAh powerhouse inside the Realme 14x 5G smartphone. Whether you're binge-watching your favourite shows, gaming for hours, or working on the go, this phone has the stamina to last all day. And when it’s time to recharge, the 45W fast-charging feature gets you back to 100% in no time, so you can spend less time plugged in and more time doing what you love.

    IP69 Dust and Water Resistance

    Life is unpredictable, and the Realme 14x 5G smartphone is built to withstand it all. With its IP69 dust and water resistance rating, you can take this phone anywhere without worrying about drops, spills, or exposure to the elements. Whether you're caught in the rain or working in a dusty environment, this phone is ready for whatever life throws at it.

    SonicWave Water Ejection

    Innovative technology is at the heart of the Realme 14x 5G smartphone. Featuring SonicWave water ejection, this smartphone can expel any unwanted moisture trapped inside, ensuring that your phone stays in top condition and continues to deliver optimal performance. It’s just another way Realme prioritises your user experience, so you can enjoy worry-free use every day.

    Dimensity 6300 5G Chipset

    Powering the Realme 14x 5G smartphone is the robust MediaTek Dimensity 6300 5G chipset, built for ultra-fast 5G connectivity. Experience seamless browsing, lag-free gaming, and smooth multitasking. With its efficient processing power, you can juggle multiple tasks without a hitch, elevating your smartphone experience to a new level of speed and responsiveness.
