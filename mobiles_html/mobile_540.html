    MediaTek Dimensity 8200 Ultimate 5G Processor Smartphone

    The MediaTek Dimensity 8200 Ultimate 5G Processor is a powerhouse chip, specifically crafted for superior gaming, delivers unmatched speed and responsiveness. It boasts of powerful CPU and GPU, supporting higher frame rates and longer performance without sacrificing performance and lower power consumption.

    Dedicated Gaming Display Chip: Pixelworks X5 Turbo

    Experience a level up in visuals with a dedicated gaming display chip. It utilizes MEMC and advanced algorithms to boost frame rates up to a staggering 90 FPS, ensuring ultra-smooth and flawless gameplay.

    Massive Storage

    8 GB LPDDR5X RAM + 256 GB UFS3.1 ROM. Enjoy lightning-fast startups and improved multitasking, even when running numerous apps in the background. Experience seamless performance at its best.

    Embrace a bold look inspired by strength and speed. The GT20 Pro's Cyber Mecha Design features a glowing Mecha Loop LED interface, a turbine blade pattern, and is crafted to offer a premium look and for other utility purpose. This phone is a statement piece for the future of mobile gaming.

    A Display Built For The Champions

    Dive into a world of stunning visuals with the GT20 Pro's bezel-less AMOLED display. Its 144 Hz refresh rate & 1500 Hz instantaneous touch sampling rate ensures ultra-smooth action, while a peak brightness of 1300 nits delivers clarity even outdoors. Plus, TUV Rheinland certification keeps your eyes comfortable during extended gaming sessions

    Capture The Sharpest Frames, Each Time

    The GT20 Pro boasts a 32 MP Selfie Camera and a 108 MP OIS Triple Rear camera set up, allowing you to capture stable photos and videos with 4K@60FPS video recording. GT20 Pro features that enable superb quality photos, even in the dark, and dual video recording, so you don’t miss out on any special moment.

    JBL Dual Speakers

    Dual Speakers with sound by JBL blends the best of hardware and software, making it easier for users to quickly pinpoint enemies based on the direction and intensity of the sound — adding a new dimension to your gaming experience.

    Clean and Pure OS

    Unleash your phone's true potential with the GT20 Pro's clean OS. Clean UI with minimal pre-installed apps+ XOS14 based on Android 14 + 2 years Major Android Upgrade and 3 Years of Security Patch update.

    Dedicated X-Boost & E-Sports Mode

    The GT 20 Pro adopts a unique Esports Mode to create a zero-disruption gaming experience. This makes the device performance equal to a gaming console, providing the ultimate gaming experience for Esports players. Esports Mode for Esports players.

    All-Day Long Battery and Ultra-fast Charging

    GT20 Pro features 45-watt fast charging and intelligent safety charging technology, providing an instant and secure charging experience. 5000 mAh fully charged battery is always ready to go.
